## Why a Family Website?

We've been meaning to do this for a while. In a world of social media feeds and algorithmic timelines, we wanted a place that was truly *ours* — somewhere to share stories, photos, and updates on our own terms.

No ads. No algorithms. Just us.

## What You'll Find Here

This site is our digital home base. Here's what we're planning:

- **Blog posts** about life, projects, and whatever catches our interest
- **Photo albums** from trips, holidays, and everyday moments
- **Events** so family and friends know what's coming up
- **Family updates** for the quick, day-to-day stuff

## The Tech Behind It

For the technically curious: this site runs on a homelab server in our living room. It's a Next.js app deployed on a K3s Kubernetes cluster, with a PostgreSQL database and MDX for blog content.

> Building things is more fun when you can share them with the people you care about.

Yes, it's overengineered for a family blog. But where's the fun in doing it the easy way?

## What's Next

We're just getting started. Expect more posts, more photos, and hopefully some interesting stories along the way. If you're family or a close friend, you'll be getting an invite to join soon.

Thanks for stopping by. Welcome to The Hudsons.
