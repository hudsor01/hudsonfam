ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup butter or margarine"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "3 eggs"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "1 3/4 cups flour"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter, add gradually sugar, egg yolks well beaten, milk, flour mixed with baking powder, and egg whites beaten stiff. Bake in a thin sheet in a moderately hot oven for 20 minutes at 375 degrees. Shape with small cutter and put together with Hazel Nut Filling. (see page 200.)"
