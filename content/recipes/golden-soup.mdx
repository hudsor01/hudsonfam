ingredients:
  - "3/4 cup cooked squash"
  - "1 pint milk"
  - "1 pint water"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "3 tablespoons flour"
  - "Salt, pepper"
  - "Onion juice"
instructions:
  - "Strain squash, add milk and water, and bring to boiling point. Rub butter and flour together, add to soup and cook until thickened, stirring constantly. Season with salt, pepper, and a few drops of onion juice. Servings, 5."
