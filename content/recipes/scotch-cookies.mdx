servings: "36"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "8 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2/3 cup sugar"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup butter or margarine"
  - "1/2 teaspoon almond extract"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients and sift three times. Work in shortening with fingers. Add flavoring. Make into a roll three inches in diameter. Let stand in a cold place until firm enough to slice. With a sharp knife cut in thin slices. Bake in a slow oven until delicately browned, in the oven for 8 minutes at 300 degrees."
