ingredients:
  - "6 apples (medium size)"
  - "1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon mixed"
  - "1 cup fruit (cherries, peaches or dates)"
  - "Pastry"
  - "1/2 cup chopped walnuts"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
instructions:
  - "Pare and core apples, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and fill with fruit. Roll out pastry dough into thin sheets, cut in squares; then wrap an apple in each square. Dip in egg and roll in chopped nuts and sugar. Bake in a moderately hot oven. Serve with cream. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes at 375 degrees."
