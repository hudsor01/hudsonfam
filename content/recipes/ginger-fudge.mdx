ingredients:
  - "1/2 tablespoon butter"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "2 cups sugar"
  - "1/4 cup crystallized ginger"
  - "1/2 cup shredded cocoanut"
instructions:
  - "Put the butter, the milk and sugar into a saucepan and cook until a soft ball forms when a little is dropped in cold water. Remove from the fire, set in cold water and when cooled beat until creamy. Add ginger and cocoanut and pour into a buttered pan. Cut in squares."
