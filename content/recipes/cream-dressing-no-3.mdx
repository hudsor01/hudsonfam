ingredients:
  - "1 teaspoon mustard"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 teaspoons flour"
  - "1 1/2 teaspoons confectioners' sugar"
  - "Speck cayenne"
  - "1 teaspoon butter"
  - "1/3 cup vinegar"
  - "1 egg yolk"
  - "1/2 cup heavy cream"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients. Add butter and vinegar. Cook until thick, stirring constantly. Add egg yolk and cook one minute. Cool, and add cream which has been stiffly beaten."
