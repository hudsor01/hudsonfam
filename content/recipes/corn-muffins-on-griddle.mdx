ingredients:
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "2 tablespoons melted shortening"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "1 cup yellow corn meal"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
instructions:
  - "Beat egg and add milk, sugar, and shortening, then add sifted dry ingredients and beat well."
  - "Bake in muffin rings on a hot griddle, filling the rings half full. Time in cooking, 25 minutes."
