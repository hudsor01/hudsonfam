cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups bread crumbs"
  - "3 cups chopped apples"
  - "2 tablespoons chopped suet"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 1/4 cups milk"
instructions:
  - "In a greased baking dish put a layer of bread crumbs and one of apples. Sprinkle with chopped suet and sugar. Repeat until dish is full. Add milk and cover with crumbs. Bake in a moderate oven for 35 minutes at 350 degrees. Serve hot with cream and sugar."
