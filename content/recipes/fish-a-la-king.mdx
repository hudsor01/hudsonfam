ingredients:
  - "1 1/4 cups milk"
  - "6 tablespoons flour"
  - "3 pimientoes"
  - "1 cup mushrooms or celery"
  - "2 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "Pinch nutmeg"
  - "2 cups fish"
instructions:
  - "Beat milk and flour together until smooth, and cook three minutes over fire."
  - "Add cut pimientoes, mushrooms, or celery cut in pieces and parboiled, and seasonings."
  - "Add fish and reheat in double boiler or chafing-dish. Servings, 6."
