cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup corn meal"
  - "1/2 cup rye meal"
  - "1/2 cup white flour"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "3 tablespoons molasses"
  - "1 teaspoon melted butter"
instructions:
  - "Mix together dry ingredients."
  - "Add the milk, molasses, and melted butter. Beat thoroughly."
  - "Steam in well greased molds for 2 1/2 hours and then bake in a moderate oven for 15 minutes at 300 degrees."
