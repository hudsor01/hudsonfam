category: "Cake"
status: "published"
order: 6802
servings: "15"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "1 pound dates (after stoning)"
  - "1 cup English walnut meats"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "4 eggs"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Have dates and nut meats as whole as possible."
  - "Sift over them flour, baking powder, salt, which have been sifted together first, then sifted again with the sugar."
  - "Beat in egg yolks and fold in whites beaten stiff."
  - "Add flavoring and bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour at 325 degrees."
