servings: "8"

ingredients:
  - "1 can shrimp"
  - "1 can tomato soup"
  - "Pinch of soda"
  - "1 can peas"
  - "1 1/2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1 1/2 tablespoons flour"
  - "1 1/2 cups milk"
  - "Salt and pepper"
instructions:
  - "Wash shrimp, remove intestinal vein, and cut each in two or three pieces. Heat tomato soup, add soda to keep from curdling the milk, add peas and shrimp, and cook a few minutes. Make a white sauce of the butter, flour, milk, seasonings, and add just before serving."
