servings: "15"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "40 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons molasses"
  - "2 tablespoons melted butter"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 teaspoon cinnamon"
  - "1/2 teaspoon clove"
  - "1/4 teaspoon ginger"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 cup sour milk"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "2 cups flour"
instructions:
  - "Add molasses and butter to sugar. Add salt and spices and stir together. Add egg well beaten, milk, soda and flour sifted together. Pour into greased tin. Bake in a moderate oven in the oven for 40 minutes at 350 degrees."
