ingredients:
  - "1 cup stoned red cherries"
  - "2 1/2 cups water"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 tablespoon gelatine"
  - "Juice of 1 orange"
instructions:
  - "Scald cherries in water, add sugar, and gelatine softened in cold water. Cool and add orange juice. Freeze to a mush, and let stand several hours. Servings, 5."
