ingredients:
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 cup boiling water"
  - "2 egg whites"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Dissolve sugar in boiling water, and boil without stirring until a soft ball is formed when a little is dropped in cold water. Have egg whites beaten until stiff and dry. Pour sugar syrup over them in a fine stream, beating constantly. Continue beating until stiff enough to hold shape. Add vanilla."
