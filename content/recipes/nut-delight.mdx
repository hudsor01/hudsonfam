title: "Nut Delight"
category: "Dessert"
status: "published"
order: 13003
servings: "5"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 cracker rolled fine"
  - "1/4 pound nuts (chopped or ground)"
  - "2 egg whites"
instructions:
  - "Mix all ingredients except egg whites together. Add these, stiffly beaten, and fold them in gently. Bake in a shallow pan in a slow oven for 20 minutes at 250 degrees. When cool, cut in squares and pile in sherbet glasses. Top with whipped cream and a cherry. Servings, 5."
