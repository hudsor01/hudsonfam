---
title: "Orange Custard Pie"
category: "Pie"
status: "published"
order: 25601
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/8 teaspoon salt"
  - "9 tablespoons sugar"
  - "4 tablespoons flour"
  - "4 tablespoons cornstarch"
  - "3 cups milk"
  - "1 orange (grated rind and sliced pulp)"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "Baked pastry shell"
instructions:
  - "Stir together salt, sugar, flour, and cornstarch. Make it into a paste with some of the milk; then add remaining milk. Add grated rind, and cook in a double boiler, stirring constantly, until it thickens."
  - "Remove from fire, and stir in the egg yolks which have been beaten with a little water. Add the juice from the sliced orange pulp and cook two minutes."
  - "Cool, fill a baked crust with the custard, and spread over it the sliced orange sections. Cover with meringue made from the two egg whites beaten stiff and one-quarter cup sugar. Brown meringue in a moderate oven in the oven for 20 minutes at 325 degrees."
