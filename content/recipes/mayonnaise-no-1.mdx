ingredients:
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 teaspoon powdered sugar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon mustard"
  - "1/4 teaspoon paprika"
  - "1 egg yolk"
  - "2 tablespoons vinegar or lemon juice"
  - "1 cup salad oil"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients, add egg yolk and blend thoroughly. Add one teaspoon of the vinegar. Add oil very slowly, beating all the time. After one-quarter cup of oil has been used, the rest may be added more rapidly. Thin with the vinegar, or lemon as necessary."
