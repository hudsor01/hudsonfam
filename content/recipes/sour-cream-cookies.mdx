ingredients:
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "3/4 cup sour cream"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "3/4 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
instructions:
  - "Beat egg well, add sugar and cream, then dry ingredients sifted together. Drop by teaspoonfuls an inch apart on a greased cooky sheet. Bake in a moderate oven for 15 minutes at 350 degrees."
