cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup cranberries"
  - "1/2 cup raisins"
  - "1/2 cup walnuts"
  - "Pinch salt"
  - "1 tablespoon flour"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 cup chopped apples"
  - "1/2 cup water"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Put cranberries, raisins, and nuts through food chopper, add salt, flour, sugar, and apples. Put into a pastry lined pie plate, pour the water over the filling, cover with a top crust, and bake in a moderately hot oven."
  - "Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 400 degrees 5 minutes, 380 degrees 25 minutes."
