cookTime: "8 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup butter or margarine"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "3 to 3 1/2 cups pastry flour"
  - "1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Cream shortening. Add sugar, egg well beaten, and three cups flour mixed and sifted with baking powder. Add vanilla. Add more flour if necessary, to make a stiff dough. Chill, roll very thin, and bake in a moderate oven until brown for 8 minutes at 325 degrees."
