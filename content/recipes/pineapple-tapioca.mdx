ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup minute tapioca"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "Pinch salt"
  - "4 cups water"
  - "1 cup crushed pineapple"
  - "Currant jelly"
instructions:
  - "Cook the tapioca, one-half cup sugar, salt, and water, in double boiler, until tapioca is clear, about forty-five minutes. Remove from fire and add pineapple and other one-half cup sugar. When cold decorate with currant jelly and serve with sweetened whipped cream. Any canned or fresh fruit may be used. Servings, 8."
