cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "2 cups bran"
  - "1 1/4 cups milk"
  - "1/2 cup molasses"
  - "1 egg"
instructions:
  - "Mix and sift flour, salt and soda. Add bran, milk, molasses, and egg well beaten. Bake in greased muffin tins in a moderate oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees."
