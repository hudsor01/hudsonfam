ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "1/2 cup thin cream"
  - "1 cup tomato soup (canned)"
  - "1/8 teaspoon soda"
  - "2 cups finely cut cheese"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "Salt"
  - "1/4 teaspoon mustard"
  - "Speck cayenne"
instructions:
  - "Melt butter, add flour, and stir well. Gradually add the cream and cook until the mixture thickens. Add tomato soup which has the soda mixed with it; then cheese, eggs slightly beaten, and seasonings. Serve on toast or toasted crackers. Servings, 6."
