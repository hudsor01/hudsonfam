cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups cherries, stoned"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 cup water"
  - "3 tablespoons cornstarch"
  - "3 tablespoons water"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Cook the cherries with the sugar and water until soft, and thicken with cornstarch mixed with water, cool, and turn into pie shell. Cover with lattice crust."
  - "Bake in a moderately hot oven, in the oven for 30 minutes at 400 degrees 5 minutes, 380 degrees 25 minutes."
