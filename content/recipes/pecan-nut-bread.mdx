ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup corn meal"
  - "1 1/2 cups white bread flour"
  - "1 3/4 cup graham flour"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 cups sweet milk"
  - "1/2 cup brown sugar"
  - "1/4 cup molasses"
  - "1 cup chopped pecan meats"
instructions:
  - "Combine the flours and add baking powder and salt. Then sift."
  - "Add other ingredients to flour, beat well."
  - "Pour into a loaf tin, and bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour at 350 degrees. A half cup of chopped dates or raisins may be added to this bread if desired."
