ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup cocoa"
  - "1 teaspoon potato flour"
  - "2 cups sugar"
  - "3/4 cup milk"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1/2 cup almond paste"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
instructions:
  - "Mix cocoa, potato flour, and sugar thoroughly. Add milk and cook until a soft ball forms in cold water. Cool, beat until creamy. Vanilla, almond paste, butter, and salt are added just before beating. Drop from teaspoon or pour into a deep square tin. This makes an excellent filling for dates."
