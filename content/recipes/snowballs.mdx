servings: "24"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2/3 cup fine granulated sugar"
  - "1/2 cup flour"
  - "1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar"
  - "6 egg whites"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Sift flour and sugar separately three times before measuring. Sift cream of tartar in flour."
  - "Beat egg whites with a pinch of salt, add sugar gradually, and the flour and flavoring."
  - "Bake in a thin sheet, in a moderate oven, in the oven for 35 minutes at 300 degrees. Cut in one-and-a-half inch squares."
  - "Dip squares in icing and roll in cocoanut."
