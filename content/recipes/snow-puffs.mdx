servings: "4"

cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "3 tablespoons cornstarch"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1/8 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/4 cup cold water"
  - "2 cups hot milk"
  - "1/4 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Mix cornstarch, sugar and salt, and add the cold water. When this is smooth, pour into hot milk and cook until it thickens. Cool slightly and add flavoring. Pour into a large mold or small individual molds and set aside until cool. Turn out of molds and surround with Chocolate Sauce (see page 162)."
