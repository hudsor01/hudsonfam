---
title: "Potato Muffins"
category: "Bread"
status: "published"
order: 5903
servings: "10"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "25-30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "4 tablespoons shortening"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 cup mashed potato"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Cream the shortening and sugar, add the egg well beaten and then the potato. Mix thoroughly. Sift flour, baking-powder and salt and add alternately with milk to first mixture."
  - "Bake in muffin tins in a moderately hot oven for 25-30 minutes at 375 degrees."
