servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "Rich pastry"
  - "6 medium apples"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "2 teaspoons cinnamon"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
instructions:
  - "Roll out pastry one-quarter inch thick. Grate apples and spread over the crust. Sprinkle with sugar, cinnamon, and bits of butter. Roll up and cut in one-inch pieces. Lay in two plates and bake in a moderate oven, basting occasionally with a thin syrup. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. Servings, 8."
