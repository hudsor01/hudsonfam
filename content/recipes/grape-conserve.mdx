ingredients:
  - "7 pounds grapes"
  - "7 pounds sugar"
  - "1 pound raisins"
  - "1 pound walnuts"
instructions:
  - "Wash grapes and remove the pulp. Cook pulp and remove the seeds by putting through a sieve. Chop skins and nuts separately. Mix pulp and sugar. Add chopped skins and whole raisins and cook until thick. Add nuts and boil five minutes more."
