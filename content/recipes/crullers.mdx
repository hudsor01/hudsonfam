---
title: "Crullers"
category: "Doughnuts"
status: "published"
order: 4701
servings: "30"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "3 minutes each"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "3 egg yolks"
  - "1 cup hot mashed potato"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "3 cups flour"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Add sugar, butter, and egg yolks to hot mashed potato, beat until light and smooth."
  - "Add milk, flour, baking-powder, salt, and vanilla."
  - "The dough should be as soft as can be handled."
  - "Cut in strips and twist for crullers."
  - "Fry in deep fat for 3 minutes each at a fat temperature of 380 degrees."
