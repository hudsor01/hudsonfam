ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup entire wheat"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "3 tablespoons sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 1/4 cups milk"
  - "1 tablespoon melted butter"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients. Beat egg slightly, add milk and melted butter. Combine mixtures and bake on hot griddle."
  - "For graham griddle cakes, use one-half cup graham flour instead of whole wheat in above recipe."
