prepTime: ""
cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 eggs"
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 cup cocoa"
  - "6 tablespoons flour"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "Pecan meats"
instructions:
  - "Beat eggs well, then beat in sugar and cocoa mixed."
  - "Fold in flour and salt sifted, a tablespoonful at a time."
  - "Drop by teaspoonfuls on a greased tin, place a half pecan on each and bake in moderate oven, in the oven for 15 minutes at 350 degrees. Remove from tin immediately after taking from oven."
