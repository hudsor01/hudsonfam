ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup shortening"
  - "1/4 cup brown sugar"
  - "1/2 cup molasses"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 1/2 teaspoons soda"
  - "1/4 cup sour milk"
  - "Flour"
instructions:
  - "Mix ingredients, dissolving soda in sour milk. Add flour to make a soft dough. Roll quite thick, cut and bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 350 degrees."
