servings: "6"

cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 pounds rump steak"
  - "Butter"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/4 teaspoon pepper"
  - "Worcestershire sauce"
instructions:
  - "Rub steak with soft butter and seasonings and let stand awhile. Just before broiling, cut deep gashes in the top with a sharp knife and fill with Worcestershire sauce. When steak is turned, cut gashes in the reverse side. Pour more sauce over steak. Broil rare. Transfer to a platter and serve with Potatoes O'Brien (see page 344)."
