title: "Clifford Tea Cookies"
category: "Cookies"

order: 8202
servings: "60"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup butter"
  - "2 cups brown sugar"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "3 1/2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup chopped nuts"
instructions:
  - "Cream the butter and sugar thoroughly. Add the eggs and continue to beat."
  - "Sift flour, soda, and salt three times and add with the nuts to the first mixture."
  - "Pack in a mold and leave in a refrigerator over night, then turn out on a board and slice as thin as possible and bake in hot oven, in the oven for 15 minutes at 400 degrees."
