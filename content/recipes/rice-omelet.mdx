title: "Rice Omelet"
category: "Cheese"
status: "published"
order: 17101
servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "3 egg yolks"
  - "2 cups cold boiled rice"
  - "3 tablespoons water"
  - "1 tablespoon sugar"
  - "Grating of nutmeg"
  - "3 egg whites"
instructions:
  - "Beat the egg yolks until lemon-colored, add the rice and water and mix thoroughly, add sugar, nutmeg, and lastly the stiffly beaten egg whites. Turn into a hot greased omelet-pan and cook like a plain omelet. When done, fold and serve on a platter garnished with little squares of jelly."
