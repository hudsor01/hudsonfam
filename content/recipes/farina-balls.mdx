ingredients:
  - "2 eggs"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1/2 cup farina"
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
instructions:
  - "Beat eggs, add softened butter and farina mixed with flour. Let stand one hour to swell. Form into balls, drop into briskly boiling soup and cook three minutes. Season very highly, serve at once."
