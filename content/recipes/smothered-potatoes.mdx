---
title: "Smothered Potatoes"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 34604
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "6 medium sized potatoes"
  - "3 tablespoons flour"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1 onion"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "Pepper"
  - "1 1/2 cups milk"
instructions:
  - "Slice the potatoes one-quarter inch thick. Place a layer of the slices on the bottom of a baking dish, sprinkle with flour, dot with butter, scrape in a little onion. Add salt and a little pepper. Fill the baking dish with layers of this kind, ending with unseasoned potatoes. Add milk and bake in the oven for 1 hour at 350 degrees."
