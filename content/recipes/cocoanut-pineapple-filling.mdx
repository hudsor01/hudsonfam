ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup water"
  - "1/2 cup pineapple juice"
  - "1/2 cup canned pineapple, cut in small pieces"
  - "2 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/8 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons cocoanut"
  - "2 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "Speck cinnamon"
instructions:
  - "Bring water and pineapple juice to boiling point. Add pineapple, cornstarch mixed with sugar and salt, and cook twenty minutes. When cool, add cocoanut, lemon juice, and cinnamon."
