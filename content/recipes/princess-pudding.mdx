title: "Princess Pudding"
category: "Dessert"
status: "published"
order: 15703
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups crumbs"
  - "2 cups milk"
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 egg yolk"
  - "1/4 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "Jelly"
  - "1 stiffly beaten egg white"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
instructions:
  - "Mix crumbs, milk, sugar and salt, and cook in a double boiler 15 minutes. Add egg yolk and vanilla. Pour into a greased baking dish and cool. Spread with jelly and dot with meringue made with the stiffly beaten egg white and sugar. Put in a moderate oven until a nice brown, in the oven for 20 minutes at 325 degrees."
