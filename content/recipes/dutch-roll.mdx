servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "2 hours"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 pounds Hamburg, half veal, half beef"
  - "1 cup bread crumbs"
  - "1/2 cup water"
  - "1 egg, beaten"
  - "1 teaspoon celery salt or dried leaves"
  - "Salt, cayenne, mustard"
  - "Salt pork"
instructions:
  - "Mix all ingredients except salt pork, seasoning well to taste, and put into pan which has been lined with two or three narrow strips of salt pork. Lay a few more thin slices of salt pork on top."
  - "Bake in a moderate oven. Serve hot or cold. Bake in the oven for 2 hours at 350 degrees."
