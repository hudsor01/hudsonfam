servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups stale bread crumbs"
  - "1 quart scalded milk"
  - "1/3 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 cup melted butter"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
instructions:
  - "Soak bread crumbs in milk, add sugar, butter, egg slightly beaten, and salt. Flavor to taste and bake in a moderate oven. Bake in the oven for 1 hour at 325 degrees. Servings, 6."
  - "Variations in Bread Pudding: Melt two squares of chocolate and add to scalded milk. Caramelize three tablespoons of sugar; dissolve in hot water to make a syrup and add to the scalded milk. Add spice and raisins."
