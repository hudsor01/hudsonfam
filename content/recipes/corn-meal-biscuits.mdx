contributor: "Grandma Hudson"
sourceNote: "From Grandma Hudson's copy of the Modern Priscilla Cook Book (Biscuits and Rolls)"
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups flour"
  - "3/4 cup corn meal"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Combine all the ingredients but the milk. Then add the milk, using enough to make a soft dough."
  - "Toss on a board lightly, roll, and cut in rounds."
  - "Brush with melted butter and fold over like Parker House rolls."
  - "Bake in a hot oven."
tags:
  - "biscuits"
  - "corn meal"
dateAdded: "2026-06-01"
---

Bake in a hot oven (about 400°F) for 30 minutes.
