cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups flour"
  - "3/4 cup corn meal"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Combine all ingredients but the milk."
  - "Then add the milk, using enough to make a soft dough."
  - "Toss on board lightly, roll and cut in rounds. Brush with melted butter and fold over like Parker House Rolls."
  - "Bake in a hot oven for 30 minutes at 400 degrees."
