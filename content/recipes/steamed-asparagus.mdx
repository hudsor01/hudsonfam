servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "forty-five minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups chopped asparagus"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "1/4 cup grated cheese"
  - "Salt, pepper, and mace to taste"
  - "Fine bread crumbs"
instructions:
  - "Boil or steam asparagus, then chop fine. Add butter and let it simmer gently for a few minutes. Beat egg yolks with milk, add cheese and stir into the asparagus. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites and season to taste. Grease a quart mold generously and line with bread crumbs. Pour in the asparagus mixture and steam forty-five minutes. Serve as main course at luncheon or supper. Servings, 4."
