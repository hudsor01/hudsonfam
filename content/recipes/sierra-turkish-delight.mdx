ingredients:
  - "2 cups apricot pulp"
  - "1 1/2 cups powdered sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon cornstarch"
  - "3 teaspoons lemon juice"
  - "Granulated sugar"
instructions:
  - "Apricot pulp is made by straining stewed apricots through a coarse strainer. Add sugar, cornstarch mixed with lemon juice and cook until stiff. Cool and pour on to a well sugared board. Allow to become firm, cut into squares and coat over with granulated sugar. Wrap in waxed paper if desired."
