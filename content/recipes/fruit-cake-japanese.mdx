servings: "18"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup butter"
  - "4 eggs"
  - "2 cups sugar"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "2 1/2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 cup chopped nuts"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1/2 pound seedless raisins"
  - "1/4 teaspoon clove"
  - "1/4 teaspoon cinnamon"
instructions:
  - "Make cake batter by creaming butter, adding beaten eggs, sugar, milk, and sifted dry ingredients."
  - "Divide in two parts; add to one portion the nuts and vanilla, and to the second portion the raisins and spice."
  - "Bake in four layers in a moderate oven for 20 minutes at 325 degrees, and put together with Japanese Filling (see page 200), using Japanese Icing for top."
