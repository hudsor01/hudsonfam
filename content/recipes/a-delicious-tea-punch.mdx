servings: "25"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "2 cups sugar"
  - "2 cups water"
  - "1 pint tea infusion"
  - "4 lemons (juice)"
  - "4 oranges (juice)"
  - "1 pint cherry juice"
  - "1 cup grated pineapple"
  - "1 quart Apollinaris or other charged water"
  - "Iced water"
instructions:
  - "Boil the sugar and the two cups of water together for ten minutes; when this is cold, add the tea infusion, using only the best tea to make this. Add the fruit juices, pineapple, and let stand a while. Then add the charged water, and enough iced water to make the desired strength."
