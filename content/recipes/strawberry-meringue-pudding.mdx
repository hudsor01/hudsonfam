---
title: "Strawberry Meringue Pudding"
category: "Dessert"
status: "published"
order: 13503
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes and 20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "2 cups milk"
  - "1 cup bread crumbs"
  - "1 cup strawberries"
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "2 egg whites"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter and sugar, add beaten yolks, milk, and crumbs. Pour into a buttered dish and bake covered, in a moderately hot oven until firm. Place strawberries over the top of pudding, add one-half cup of sugar and spread with meringue made from stiffly beaten whites and one-quarter cup sugar. Bake in a moderate oven to set meringue. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes and 20 minutes at 375 and 325 degrees. Serve cold with cream."
