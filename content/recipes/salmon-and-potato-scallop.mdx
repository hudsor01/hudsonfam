servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "1 cup flaked salmon"
  - "1/2 cup boiled onions"
  - "1 quart mashed potato"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "Pepper"
instructions:
  - "Make a paste of the flour and butter, add the milk, and cook until thick. Then add the salmon and onions."
  - "Line a baking dish with mashed potato and fill with the creamed salmon. Beat the eggs, add the milk, salt, and pepper, and pour over the potato and salmon."
  - "Bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 375 degrees."
