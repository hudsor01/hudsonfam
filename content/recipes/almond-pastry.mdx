ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups pastry flour"
  - "3 tablespoons ground almonds"
  - "3 tablespoons sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 cup shortening"
  - "1 egg"
  - "Water"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients and rub in shortening. Add beaten egg and enough water to hold paste together."
  - "Roll and bake in a hot oven."
  - "Almond pastry may be used for custard pies, lemon pies, fresh or dried fruit pies, or for tarts to be filled with jelly, marmalade, fruit, or whipped cream mixtures."
