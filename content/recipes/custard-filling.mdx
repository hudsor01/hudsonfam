ingredients:
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
  - "Speck salt"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1/4 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Heat milk. Cream butter, mix with flour, and add milk. Cook ten minutes. Add sugar, salt, and slightly beaten egg. Cook in double boiler until thick. Cool, and add vanilla."
