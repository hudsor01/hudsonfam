order: 26903
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups chicken broth"
  - "3 tablespoons flour"
  - "3 tablespoons butter"
  - "Salt"
  - "Speck pepper"
  - "2 cups diced chicken"
  - "2 cups fresh or canned corn"
  - "1/8 teaspoon celery salt, or 1/2 cup celery"
  - "Bread crumbs"
instructions:
  - "Make a sauce of broth, flour, butter, salt, and pepper."
  - "Put chicken and corn in baking dish in alternate layers, sprinkling each with celery salt or diced, parboiled celery. Add sauce, sprinkle with soft bread crumbs, and bake in a moderately hot oven, in the oven for 30 minutes at 375 degrees."
