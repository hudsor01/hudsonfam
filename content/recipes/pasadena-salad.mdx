ingredients:
  - "3/4 cup shrimp (1 small can)"
  - "1 1/2 cups celery"
  - "1/2 cup radishes"
  - "1/2 cup peas"
  - "Salt and pepper"
  - "1 teaspoon tarragon vinegar"
  - "1/2 cup mayonnaise"
  - "1/4 cup nuts"
instructions:
  - "Cut shrimp in small pieces. Dice celery and slice radishes. Combine shrimp and vegetables, adding a little salt and pepper. Pour into a salad bowl, add vinegar, and spread mayonnaise over top. Sprinkle with chopped nuts and garnish with tiny hearts of lettuce. Servings, 6."
