servings: "6"

ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons gelatine"
  - "1/2 cup cold water"
  - "6 tart apples"
  - "1/2 lemon (rind)"
  - "Water or sweet cider"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 lemon (juice)"
instructions:
  - "Soak gelatine in cold water. Cook quartered apples with lemon rind and water or sweet cider to cover. Press through a sieve and add sugar and lemon juice. Add gelatine and when dissolved pour into mold. Serve with plain or whipped cream. Servings, 6."
