ingredients:
  - "1 egg white"
  - "1/2 cup powdered sugar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1 egg yolk"
  - "1/2 cup whipped cream or"
  - "1 1/2 tablespoons milk"
instructions:
  - "Beat egg white until stiff. Beat in gradually the powdered sugar and when smooth and light add vanilla and well beaten yolk. Then stir in cream or milk. Serve at once."
