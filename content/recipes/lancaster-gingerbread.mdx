servings: "12"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "25 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "3 cups flour"
  - "2 teaspoons soda"
  - "1/2 teaspoon ginger"
  - "1/2 teaspoon clove"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 cup shortening"
  - "1 cup molasses"
  - "1 cup sour milk"
  - "2 eggs"
instructions:
  - "Mix and sift dry ingredients. Melt shortening, add molasses, sour milk, and eggs. Beat all together until light and smooth. Bake in angel-cake pan in a moderate oven in the oven for 25 minutes at 350 degrees."
