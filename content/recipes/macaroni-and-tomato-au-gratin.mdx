title: "Macaroni And Tomato Au Gratin"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 20303
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups macaroni"
  - "1 pint tomato"
  - "1 tablespoon sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 teaspoon celery salt"
  - "Pepper"
  - "1/2 cup cheese"
  - "1/2 cup crumbs"
instructions:
  - "Boil macaroni in salted water until nearly tender, drain, and rinse. Season tomato with sugar, salt, celery salt, a little pepper, and pour over the macaroni. Sprinkle with cheese and crumbs, and bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour at 350 degrees."
