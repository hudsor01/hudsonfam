ingredients:
  - "2 cups finely chopped meat"
  - "3 tablespoons butter"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 teaspoon mustard"
  - "2 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "Speck pepper"
instructions:
  - "Any two or more kinds of cold meats may be used. Cream butter and add seasonings, add to meat, and rub to a paste. Pack in tumblers that have been wet in cold water, pour over a little melted suet, and put in a cold place. Serve cold, cut in slices."
