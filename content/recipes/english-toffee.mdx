ingredients:
  - "1/2 pound white sugar"
  - "1/4 pound brown sugar"
  - "3/4 pound corn syrup"
  - "1 1/4 cups thin cream"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "3 ounces butter"
  - "Flavoring"
  - "Nuts or fruit"
instructions:
  - "Mix all ingredients except butter, which is added just before removing from stove. Cook until it forms a hard ball when tried in cold water, add one-half teaspoon of lemon or any flavoring preferred, and one cup of chopped walnuts, peanuts, or fruit, or one-half cup of shredded cocoanut. Pour into a buttered pan and when cold cut into long narrow bars and wrap in waxed paper."
