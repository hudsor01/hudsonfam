ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 tablespoon cornstarch"
  - "1 cup boiling water"
  - "Salt"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "Nutmeg"
instructions:
  - "Mix the sugar with the cornstarch, add the boiling water and a few grains of salt and boil until thick and clear. Beat in the butter, the lemon juice, and a little nutmeg. A bit of grated lemon rind may be added. Serve hot."
