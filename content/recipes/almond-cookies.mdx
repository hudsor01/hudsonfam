title: "Almond Cookies"
category: "Cookies"
status: "published"
order: 8001
servings: "30"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "10 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup butter"
  - "1/4 cup almond paste"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 egg yolk"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "3 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter. Rub almond paste with the sugar until crumbly, then add to the butter."
  - "Add beaten egg, milk, sifted dry ingredients and beat well. Add enough more flour to form soft dough."
  - "Roll one-fourth inch thick, cut with a doughnut-cutter and bake in moderately hot oven, in the oven for 10 minutes at 375 degrees."
