cookTime: "3 minutes each"
ingredients:
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "3/4 cup milk"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "1/2 cup hot mashed potato"
  - "Flour"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/4 teaspoon spice"
  - "1/2 teaspoon lemon extract"
instructions:
  - "Add sugar, milk, and butter to hot potato and stir in enough flour mixed and sifted with other dry ingredients, to make a soft dough. Roll, cut, and fry in deep fat for 3 minutes each at a fat temperature of 380 degrees."
