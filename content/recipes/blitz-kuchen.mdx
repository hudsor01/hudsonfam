title: "Blitz Kuchen"
category: "Cake"
status: "published"
order: 6502
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 1/2 cups flour"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 cup butter or margarine"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "4 tablespoons crushed nuts"
instructions:
  - "Sift sugar, baking powder, flour, and salt into a bowl. Add butter or margarine and rub into dry ingredients. Add beaten eggs and milk, and if necessary more flour to make a rather stiff batter. Spread about one inch deep in greased pans. Sprinkle top with sugar and crushed nuts. Bake in a moderate oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees."
