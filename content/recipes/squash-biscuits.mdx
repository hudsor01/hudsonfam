ingredients:
  - "2 cups sifted flour"
  - "3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1 1/2 tablespoons lard"
  - "1 1/2 cups cooked strained squash"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients."
  - "Rub in shortening, add squash, and milk."
  - "Pat and roll lightly on floured board. Shape with biscuit cutter and place in greased pan."
  - "Bake in the oven for 15 minutes at 400 degrees."
