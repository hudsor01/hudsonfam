ingredients:
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 cup corn syrup"
  - "1/2 cup cream or evaporated milk"
  - "1/2 cup water"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "2 cups puffed rice"
instructions:
  - "Boil all ingredients, except rice, together without stirring until a firm ball forms in cold water. Remove from fire, flavor, and stir in lightly and quickly crisp puffed rice. Turn at once into a greased pan and spread to three-fourths inch in thickness. Cut in squares when cool."
