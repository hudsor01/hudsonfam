servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup heavy cream"
  - "8 egg yolks"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/4 teaspoon paprika"
instructions:
  - "Beat cream until stiff, add egg yolks and beat until well blended. Add salt. Melt butter in omelet-pan and turn in egg mixture. Cook slowly until brown on bottom, and place in a moderate oven until firm for 15 minutes at 325 degrees. Sprinkle with paprika."
