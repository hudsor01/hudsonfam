cookTime: "40 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "Salt, pepper, cayenne"
  - "2 cups mashed parsnip"
instructions:
  - "Add egg yolks slightly beaten, milk and seasonings to mashed parsnip. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites, pour in buttered soufflé cups, and bake until firm in a moderate oven. Bake in the oven for 40 minutes at 300 degrees."
