title: "Stuffed Tomatoes, Southern Style"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 35403
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "6 good sized tomatoes"
  - "1 cup corn"
  - "2 tablespoons green pepper"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 tablespoon onion"
  - "1/8 teaspoon pepper"
  - "1 cup bread crumbs"
  - "6 slices of bacon"
instructions:
  - "Cut a slice from the stem end of each tomato and scoop out the seeds and a portion of the pulp."
  - "Mix the tomato pulp with the rest of the ingredients. Fill the tomatoes, heaping slightly, and lay a slice of bacon on each."
  - "Stand in a baking pan with one-half cup of water and bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees."
