cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "4 tablespoons butter"
  - "1/4 cup flour"
  - "2 cups milk"
  - "1 1/2 cups flaked fish"
  - "1/4 teaspoon celery salt or dried celery leaves"
  - "1 teaspoon grated onion"
  - "2 teaspoons salt"
  - "Pepper"
  - "1/4 cup bread crumbs"
instructions:
  - "Melt butter, add flour and mix to a smooth paste. Add milk and cook until thick, stirring all the time; then add fish and seasonings. Put into individual ramekins, sprinkle with crumbs and bake in a moderately hot oven for 20 minutes at 375 degrees."
