ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "2 cups apples (chopped)"
instructions:
  - "Sift flour, baking powder and salt and add to well beaten eggs, and milk. Chop apples and add. Bake in individual muffin tins in a hot oven. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 400 degrees. Servings, 12."
