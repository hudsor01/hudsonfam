ingredients:
  - "1 cup salt"
  - "1/2 peck ripe tomatoes (chopped fine)"
  - "1/2 peck green tomatoes (chopped fine)"
  - "6 red sweet peppers (chopped)"
  - "6 green sweet peppers (chopped)"
  - "12 small onions (chopped)"
  - "2 quarts vinegar"
  - "3 pints brown sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon whole cloves"
  - "1 tablespoon mustard seed"
  - "1 tablespoon cinnamon"
instructions:
  - "Add salt to tomatoes, let stand four hours, drain, and add remaining ingredients. Boil three-quarters of an hour. Pack in hot sterilized jars and seal or keep in a crock in a cool place."
