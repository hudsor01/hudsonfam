---
title: "Currant Drops"
category: "Cookies"
status: "published"
order: 8501
servings: "36"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "10 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/3 cup butter or other shortening"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 lemon (grated rind)"
  - "2 tablespoons milk"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 cup currants"
instructions:
  - "Cream shortening, add sugar gradually, beating all the time, then the beaten egg and lemon rind. Add milk and flour mixed and sifted with baking powder and salt. Save out a little of the flour to mix with the currants, which have been washed, and add them last. Drop on greased tin and sprinkle with sugar. Bake in a hot oven for 10 minutes at 400 degrees."
