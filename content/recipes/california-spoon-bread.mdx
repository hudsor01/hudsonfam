  - "1 teaspoon salt"

  - "1 cup boiled rice"
  - "8 long or 16 short slices of bacon"
instructions:
  - "Scald the milk in a double boiler and pour it on the beaten eggs. Return to the boiler and cook until it coats the spoon."
  - "Add the boiling water and salt. Stir in the corn meal and cook five minutes."
  - "Put the custard in a buttered baking dish (glass if possible), then add alternately spoonfuls of mush and rice until the baking dish is full."
  - "Arrange the bacon across the top of the dish. If the slices are long they will cross in the center; if short, they can radiate from the center."
  - "Bake in a moderate oven."
