ingredients:
  - "1 cup heavy cream"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
  - "1/8 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 cup fresh roasted peanuts"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "6 slices pineapple"
instructions:
  - "Whip cream, add sugar, salt, ground peanuts, and vanilla. Chill. Arrange pineapple on lettuce and heap dressing on top. Garnish with pieces of red cherries. Servings, 6."
