servings: "2"

ingredients:
  - "1 onion"
  - "2 tablespoons fat"
  - "2 cups tomato"
  - "Juice of 1/2 lemon"
  - "1 cup meat cut in cubes"
  - "3/4 teaspoon curry powder"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Cook the onion in the fat. Add tomato, lemon juice, cooked meat cut in cubes, and curry powder moistened with cold water. Simmer twenty minutes."
  - "Stir in the cold milk and reheat. Serve with a border of boiled rice and fresh banana."
