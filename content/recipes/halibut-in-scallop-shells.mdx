ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "1 1/2 cups cooked halibut"
  - "Onion slices"
  - "Mashed potato"
instructions:
  - "Make a white sauce of the butter, flour, and milk. Season to taste and add the baked halibut."
  - "Fill scallop shells and cover with the halibut mixture. Decorate each with mashed potato put through a pastry tube, garnish with onion slices, and heat thoroughly."
