title: "Orange Blossom Cakes"
category: "Cake"
status: "published"
order: 7902
servings: "18"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/4 cup shortening"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "2 egg yolks (unbeaten)"
  - "3/4 cup milk"
  - "1 1/2 cups flour"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon orange flavoring"
  - "1 egg white (stiffly beaten)"
instructions:
  - "Cream shortening well and gradually work in sugar."
  - "Add the other ingredients, except egg white, in order given. Beat well to get a light fluffy mixture, fold in egg white and bake in a moderately hot oven, in muffin tins or paper cases, in the oven for 20 minutes at 375 degrees."
