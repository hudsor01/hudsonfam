servings: ""
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 tablespoon sugar"
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "3/4 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Mix and sift flour with baking powder, salt and sugar."
  - "Work in shortening, and mix to a soft dough with the milk."
  - "Roll out on a floured board to one-third inch in thickness. Cut in rounds and spread with melted butter. Fold one half over on to the other half and bake in the oven for 15 minutes at 450 degrees."
