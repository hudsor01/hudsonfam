ingredients:
  - "1 tablespoon gelatine"
  - "1/2 cup cream"
  - "1 ounce cream cheese"
  - "1 tablespoon Roquefort cheese"
  - "Salt, cayenne"
  - "Parmesan cheese"
instructions:
  - "Soften gelatine with one tablespoon water and dissolve over hot water. Add cream, cream cheese, Roquefort cheese, and seasonings, and stir until well mixed. Wet a mold and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, pour in the cheese mixture and chill. Serve on lettuce with pineapple and mayonnaise, or as a salad accompaniment or a sandwich filling."
