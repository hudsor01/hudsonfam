servings: "10"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup butter"
  - "2 cups sugar"
  - "4 squares chocolate"
  - "4 eggs"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "3 cups flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Cream the butter, add the sugar gradually and mix thoroughly, partly dissolving the sugar."
  - "Melt the chocolate over hot water, and add it to the creamed butter mixture."
  - "Add the beaten egg yolks; then the milk and flour alternately. The baking powder should be sifted with the last cupful of flour."
  - "Add the flavoring, and then fold in the beaten whites of eggs."
  - "Bake in layers in a moderate oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. For the filling and frosting use Quick Icing (see page 197)."
