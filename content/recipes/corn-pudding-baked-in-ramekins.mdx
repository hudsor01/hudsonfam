title: "Corn Pudding Baked In Ramekins"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"

servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "10 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1/2 tablespoon flour"
  - "1/4 cup rich milk or cream"
  - "1 cup corn (canned or fresh)"
  - "1/4 cup buttered bread crumbs"
instructions:
  - "Melt butter, add flour, blend, and add milk. Cook three minutes. Add corn, mix, and put in ramekins. Sprinkle tops with buttered bread crumbs and brown in a hot oven in the oven for 10 minutes at 400 degrees. Servings, 4."
