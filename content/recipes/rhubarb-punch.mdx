ingredients:
  - "3 pounds rhubarb"
  - "1 quart water"
  - "2 1/2 cups sugar"
  - "1 1/2 cups orange juice"
  - "1 cup lemon juice"
  - "3/4 cup shredded pineapple"
  - "3 quarts iced water"
instructions:
  - "Cut the rhubarb into small pieces and cook in the water until tender (about twenty-five minutes), then strain. There should be two quarts of juice."
  - "Add the sugar while the juice is hot and stir until dissolved."
  - "Add the fruit juices, and when cooled add the iced water."
  - "Serve with half slices of lemon and orange."
