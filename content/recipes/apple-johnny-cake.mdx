---
title: "Apple Johnny Cake"
category: "Bread"
status: "published"
order: 5802
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups corn meal"
  - "3/8 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 teaspoon cream of tartar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 1/2 cups milk"
  - "2 1/2 cups chopped tart apples"
instructions:
  - "Mix and sift dry ingredients and add milk and apples. Beat well and turn into a well-greased shallow tin."
  - "Bake in a hot oven for 30 minutes at 400 degrees. If the apples are not very tart, less sugar will be needed."
