servings: "10"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "40 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/4 cup butter"
  - "1 cup water"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "4 eggs"
  - "Plain pastry"
  - "Peach halves"
  - "Whipped cream"
instructions:
  - "Heat butter and water to boiling point. Add flour and stir until a ball is formed. Remove from stove and beat in eggs, one at a time. Cut pastry in rounds. Around the edge of each pipe the first mixture, using a pastry tube. Bake in a moderate oven. In centre of each tart place one-half peach. Garnish with whipped cream. Bake in the oven for 40 minutes at 350 degrees."
