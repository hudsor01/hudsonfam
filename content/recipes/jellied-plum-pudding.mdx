servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons gelatine"
  - "1 cup cold water"
  - "2 cups boiling water"
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 cup lemon juice"
  - "1 cup chopped dried fruits"
  - "1/3 cup shredded cocoanut"
instructions:
  - "Soften gelatine in cold water and dissolve in boiling water. Add sugar, lemon juice, dried fruits, and cocoanut. Dates, chopped raisins, and small whole raisins make a very satisfactory fruit combination. Turn into a ring mold to harden. When serving fill the centre with sweetened whipped cream. Sprinkle with nuts. Servings, 6."
