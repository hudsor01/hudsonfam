servings: "36"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "3 minutes each"
ingredients:
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "4 to 5 cups flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
instructions:
  - "Beat eggs very light, continue beating while adding the sugar."
  - "Mix and sift two cups of the flour with the other dry ingredients."
  - "Add milk alternately with the sifted flour."
  - "Add the flavoring, melted butter, and beat hard."
  - "Add more flour to make a dough as soft as can be handled."
  - "Roll, cut out and fry in deep fat for 3 minutes each at a fat temperature of 380 degrees."
