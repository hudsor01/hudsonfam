cookTime: "40 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups water"
  - "3/4 cups corn meal"
  - "2 teaspoons salt"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "3/4 cup milk"
  - "3/4 cup mashed potatoes"
  - "1 tablespoon shortening"
instructions:
  - "Mix the corn meal, salt, and water, and boil five minutes."
  - "Beat the eggs slightly, add milk, mashed potatoes, and shortening. Combine with the mush mixture."
  - "Beat well and bake in a greased baking dish in the oven for 40 minutes at 325 degrees."
  - "Serve from the same dish with a spoon."
