servings: ""
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "2 hours"
ingredients:
  - "Duck"
  - "2 slices fat salt pork"
  - "5 slices carrot"
  - "1/2 onion (sliced)"
  - "2 sprigs thyme"
  - "1 sprig parsley"
  - "1 bay leaf"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "Boiling water"
instructions:
  - "Dress and clean duck and steam one hour. Stuff and truss."
  - "In a frying-pan try out the fat salt pork, cut one-quarter inch thick. Add carrot cut in small cubes, and onion, thyme, parsley, bay leaf, and cook ten minutes. Then add butter and brown the duck in it."
  - "Place on trivet in a deep pan, add the contents of the frying-pan, rinse it out with one cup of boiling water, and add another cup of boiling water."
  - "Cover and bake in a moderate oven until tender, basting often and adding more water if needed. Serve with gravy made from stock in pan. Bake in the oven for 2 hours at 350 degrees."
