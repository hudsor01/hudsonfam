ingredients:
  - "2 cups sour milk"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 tablespoon sugar"
  - "1/2 cup corn meal"
  - "1/2 cup bran"
  - "1/2 cup flour"
  - "2 cups steel cut oatmeal"
  - "2 tablespoons melted butter"
instructions:
  - "Mix milk and soda thoroughly. Mix all dry ingredients and combine the two mixtures. Add melted butter and pour into greased muffin pans."
  - "Bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour at 350 degrees."
