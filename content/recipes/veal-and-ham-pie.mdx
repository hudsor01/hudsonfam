ingredients:
  - "1 pound cooked veal"
  - "1 tablespoon poultry seasoning"
  - "1/8 teaspoon nutmeg"
  - "Pepper and salt to taste"
  - "1/4 pound boiled ham"
  - "1 cup cold water"
  - "1/2 teaspoon minced lemon peel"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Cut veal in very small pieces, put a layer in a pie dish, sprinkle with seasonings, and put in a layer of thinly sliced ham. Repeat until all of the meat is used and add water. Cover with a rich pastry and brush over with beaten egg. Bake in a moderately hot oven in the oven for 1 hour at 375 degrees. Serve either hot or cold."
