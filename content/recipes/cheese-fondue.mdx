servings: "4"

cookTime: "45 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/3 cups soft stale bread crumbs"
  - "1 1/3 cups hot milk"
  - "1 1/3 cups grated cheese or"
  - "1 cup cheese cut in small pieces"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "4 eggs"
instructions:
  - "Mix bread crumbs, milk, cheese, salt, and butter. Add egg yolks thoroughly beaten. Fold in whites of eggs beaten until stiff. Pour into a greased baking dish and bake in a moderate oven until firm for 45 minutes at 325 degrees."
