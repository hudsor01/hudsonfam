ingredients:
  - "1 pound Concord grapes"
  - "4 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "1/3 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 teaspoon cinnamon"
instructions:
  - "Wash the grapes thoroughly and pick them from the stem. Add just enough water to keep them from burning, and boil until they are soft enough to press through a colander or coarse sieve. Add the other ingredients and boil until thick. Pour into hot sterilized jars and seal."
