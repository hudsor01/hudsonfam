title: "Lady Fingers"
category: "Cake"
status: "published"
order: 7901
servings: "20"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "8 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "6 egg whites"
  - "2/3 cup powdered sugar"
  - "4 egg yolks"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "2/3 cup flour"
  - "1/3 teaspoon salt"
instructions:
  - "Beat egg whites until stiff and dry, add gradually powdered sugar, and continue the beating."
  - "Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored, fold into first mixture then add vanilla and fold in flour mixed with salt."
  - "Shape close together on an unbuttered paper placed on a tin sheet, using a pastry-bag and tube. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and bake in the oven for 8 minutes at 325 degrees."
