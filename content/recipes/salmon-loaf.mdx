servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "1 large can salmon"
  - "4 tablespoons melted butter"
  - "1 cup fine bread crumbs"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 teaspoon prepared mustard"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 teaspoon pepper"
  - "2 tablespoons chopped parsley"
  - "1 cup boiled vermicelli"
instructions:
  - "Chop the salmon and add the butter, bread crumbs, egg, and seasonings."
  - "Mix, pour into a greased mold, and steam one hour."
  - "Turn out on a platter, garnish with the vermicelli, and serve with White Sauce (see page 240)."
