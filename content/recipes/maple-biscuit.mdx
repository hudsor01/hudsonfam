servings: ""
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "3/4 cup milk"
  - "Maple sugar"
instructions:
  - "Sift dry ingredients together, cut or rub in shortening, add milk gradually to make soft dough that can be handled."
  - "Roll out to one-half inch thickness, and cut in rounds."
  - "Brush half of them generously with softened butter, spread thickly with finely grated maple sugar and put together in pairs."
  - "Place in greased pan, and bake in hot oven for 20 minutes at 450 degrees."
