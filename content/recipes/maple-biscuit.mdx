sourceNote: "From Grandma Hudson's copy of the Modern Priscilla Cook Book (Biscuits and Rolls)"
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "3/4 cup milk"
  - "Maple sugar"
instructions:
  - "Sift the dry ingredients together, then cut or rub in the shortening."
  - "Add the milk gradually to make a soft dough that can be handled."
  - "Roll out to 1/2-inch thickness and cut in rounds."
  - "Brush half of them generously with softened butter, spread thickly with finely grated maple sugar, and put together in pairs."
  - "Place in a greased pan and bake in a hot oven (about 450°F) for 20 minutes."
