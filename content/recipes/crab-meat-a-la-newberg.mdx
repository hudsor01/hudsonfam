ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups crab meat"
  - "1/4 cup butter"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "Speck nutmeg"
  - "1/2 cup thin cream"
  - "2 egg yolks"
instructions:
  - "Cut crab meat in pieces. Melt butter, add crab meat, and cook three minutes. Add seasonings, cream, and egg yolks slightly beaten. Cook until thickened, stirring constantly."
