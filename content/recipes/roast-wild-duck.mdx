cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 wild duck"
  - "Peanut Stuffing (see page 274)"
  - "Fat"
  - "Salt and pepper"
  - "Currant jelly or orange sauce"
  - "Parsley"
instructions:
  - "Dress and clean a wild duck, stuff with Peanut Stuffing (see page 274) and truss securely."
  - "Place on a rack in roasting pan, brush lightly with fat, and sprinkle with salt and pepper."
  - "If wanted rare, bake thirty minutes in a hot oven, basting every five minutes with the fat which will drip out into the pan."
  - "Cook longer if desired, reducing the temperature."
  - "Put on a platter, remove the string and skewers, and garnish with parsley."
  - "With the duck serve currant jelly or orange sauce. Temperature of oven, 425 degrees."
