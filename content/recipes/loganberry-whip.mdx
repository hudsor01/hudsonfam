category: "Dessert"
status: "published"
order: 12801
servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "1 teaspoon gelatine"
  - "1 tablespoon water"
  - "1 cup strained loganberries"
  - "1 egg white"
  - "1 cup powdered sugar"
  - "1 tablespoon lemon juice"
instructions:
  - "Soften gelatine in cold water and dissolve over hot water. Put all ingredients in a bowl and beat with a wire whisk until stiff enough to hold shape. Serve very cold. Any strained berry or fruit pulp may be used in place of loganberries. Servings, 4."
