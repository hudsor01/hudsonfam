ingredients:
  - "1 tablespoon cornstarch"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "1 teaspoon mustard (prepared)"
  - "1 teaspoon tomato catsup"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 teaspoon paprika"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 tablespoon melted butter"
instructions:
  - "Mix cornstarch with milk, add mustard, catsup, salt, and paprika and cook in double boiler until cornstarch taste has disappeared. Add well beaten egg and cook three minutes longer. Add butter and serve."
