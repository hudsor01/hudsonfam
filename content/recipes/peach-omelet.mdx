title: "Peach Omelet"
category: "Eggs"
status: "published"
order: 17003
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "5 eggs"
  - "5 tablespoons syrup from canned peaches"
  - "Pinch salt"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "2 cups sliced canned peaches"
  - "Powdered sugar"
instructions:
  - "Beat eggs slightly, add peach syrup and salt. Melt butter in a frying-pan and turn in egg mixture. Cook at a low temperature until browned underneath, about ten minutes, lifting occasionally to let the top part run under. Spread with sliced peaches. Fold and sprinkle with powdered sugar."
