title: "Pea Soufflé"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 34001
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "3 tablespoons butter"
  - "3 tablespoons flour"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "2 cups peas"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 teaspoon finely minced onion"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "Speck pepper"
instructions:
  - "Melt butter, add flour, and blend thoroughly. Add milk gradually and stir constantly until thickened. Add peas which have been forced through a sieve, egg yolks slightly beaten, and seasonings. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Bake in a moderate oven. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 325 degrees."
