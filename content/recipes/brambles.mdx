ingredients:
  - "1 cup raisins"
  - "1/2 cup dates"
  - "1/2 cup nuts"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "4 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Chop fruit and nuts, add sugar, and lemon juice. Roll pastry thin and cut in squares. Put a tablespoon or more of the mixture on a square. Wet the edges and fold over to form a triangle. Prick holes with a fork in upper sides. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 400 degrees."
