category: "Pie"

order: 25201
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "40 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 medium sized carrots"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 1/2 cups milk"
  - "1/2 teaspoon ginger"
  - "1/2 teaspoon cinnamon"
  - "Speck salt"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Cook carrots until tender and transparent. Mash. Add sugar, beaten eggs, milk, spices, and salt. Mix and pour into raw pastry shell."
  - "Bake in a moderately hot oven, in the oven for 40 minutes at 400 degrees 5 minutes and 360 degrees 35 minutes."
