title: "Sour Milk Biscuits"
category: "Bread"
status: "published"
order: 4402
servings: ""
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour (bread)"
  - "1 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon soda"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "About 7/8 cup sour milk (thick)"
instructions:
  - "Mix and sift dry ingredients and work in shortening."
  - "Add liquid gradually, making a dough as soft as can be handled."
  - "Turn on to a floured board, pat and roll to about one-half inch in thickness, cut, place in a greased pan, and bake in the oven for 15 minutes at 450 degrees."
