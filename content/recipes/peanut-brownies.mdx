ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup peanut butter"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1/4 cup milk"
  - "1 square chocolate"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/4 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 cup flour"
instructions:
  - "Cream peanut butter and butter together. Add sugar, egg well beaten, milk, and chocolate melted. Then add mixed and sifted dry ingredients. Drop by spoonfuls on greased baking sheets. Bake in a moderately hot oven for 10 minutes at 375 degrees."
