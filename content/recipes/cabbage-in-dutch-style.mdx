title: "Cabbage In Dutch Style"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 32903
servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "one-half hour"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup boiled cabbage"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce"
  - "1 teaspoon salt, speck pepper and paprika"
  - "1 cup mashed potatoes"
  - "4 slices crisp bacon"
instructions:
  - "Chop the cabbage and place in frying-pan with the butter, Worcestershire sauce, and seasonings. Stir until heated through. Add the potato and mix well. Press into a greased mold and steam for one-half hour. Turn out on a hot platter and surround with tomato sauce. Garnish with the bacon. Servings, 4."
