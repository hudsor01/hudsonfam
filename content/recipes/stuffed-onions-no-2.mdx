servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "45 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "6 onions"
  - "Sausage meat"
  - "1/2 cup minced celery"
  - "2 tablespoons minced onion"
  - "2 cups white sauce"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "4 teaspoons flour"
instructions:
  - "Choose onions of uniform size. Remove skins and parboil ten minutes. Remove centre, and fill with a mixture of sausage meat, minced celery, and onion. Pour white sauce over the stuffed onions, sprinkle with salt and flour. Bake in a moderate oven. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes at 350 degrees."
