servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "12 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 teaspoons sugar"
  - "1/4 cup butter"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients, cut in butter and add milk gradually. Toss on floured board and divide into two parts. Pat, roll out, and bake in a hot oven for 12 minutes at 400 degrees. Spread with butter and place sweetened berries between layers and on top."
