title: "Stuffed Tomatoes, Baked"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 35401
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "6 medium sized tomatoes"
  - "1/2 cup soft bread crumbs"
  - "1/2 cup finely chopped cold cooked chicken or veal"
  - "Salt"
  - "Pepper"
  - "1/4 cup buttered crumbs"
instructions:
  - "Wipe the tomatoes and remove a thin slice from the stem end. Remove the pulp."
  - "Mix with the soft bread crumbs, meat, and seasoning. Refill the tomato shells and sprinkle the tops with buttered crumbs."
  - "Place in a baking pan and bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees, until the tomato is cooked. Nuts may be used instead of the finely chopped meat."
