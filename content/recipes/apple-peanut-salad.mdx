ingredients:
  - "1 cup celery"
  - "2 cups apple"
  - "1 tablespoon peanut butter"
  - "4 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "1 teaspoon sugar"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
instructions:
  - "Dice celery and apples. Blend peanut butter and lemon juice. Add sugar and salt, pour over apples and celery. Chill thoroughly and serve on crisp lettuce leaves. Servings, 6."
