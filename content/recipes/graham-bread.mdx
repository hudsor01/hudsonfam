servings: ""
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "2 teaspoons salt"
  - "3 tablespoons molasses"
  - "5 cups water, or milk scalded and cooled"
  - "1 yeast cake dissolved in 1/4 cup lukewarm water"
  - "6 cups white flour"
  - "1 tablespoon shortening"
  - "Graham flour"
instructions:
  - "Mix all ingredients, except graham flour, to smooth consistency and let stand in a warm place until light. Stir in enough graham flour to make a dough just stiff enough to handle."
  - "Mold and put into pans to rise. When loaves have doubled their bulk bake in a moderately hot oven for 1 hour at 380 degrees."
