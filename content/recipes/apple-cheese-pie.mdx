title: "Apple Cheese Pie"
category: "Pie"
status: "published"
order: 25102
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup cottage cheese"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1/8 teaspoon salt"
  - "Cream or rich milk"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "Pastry"
  - "1 1/2 cups apples"
  - "Sugar"
  - "Speck nutmeg and cinnamon"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Beat eggs, add cottage cheese, sugar, salt, cream, and vanilla. Line plate with pastry, put in apples, very thinly sliced, and mixed with sugar and spice. The amount of sugar will depend upon the tartness of the apples. Cover with cheese mixture."
  - "Bake in a moderately hot oven until cheese is set, in the oven for 35 minutes at 400 degrees 10 minutes and 350 degrees 25 minutes."
