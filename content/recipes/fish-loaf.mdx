servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "3 1/2 tablespoons butter"
  - "3 1/2 tablespoons flour"
  - "2 cups milk or fish stock"
  - "2/3 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/4 cup soft bread crumbs"
  - "2 cups cooked fish"
  - "2 eggs"
instructions:
  - "Melt butter or any suitable fat, add flour, and when smooth stir in liquid."
  - "Cook until thickened, add seasonings and crumbs and cool."
  - "Remove bones and skin from fish, add to sauce and mix well."
  - "Stir in beaten yolks of eggs and fold in stiffly beaten whites."
  - "Turn into a greased baking dish and bake in moderately hot oven until firm."
  - "Serve with Cheese Sauce (see page 235). Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 375 degrees. Servings, 6."
