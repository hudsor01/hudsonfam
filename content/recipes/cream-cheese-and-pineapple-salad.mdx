ingredients:
  - "1 slice pineapple"
  - "Lettuce"
  - "1 tablespoon cream cheese"
  - "1/2 teaspoon chopped chives"
  - "Pimiento"
instructions:
  - "Place pineapple on heart leaves of lettuce. Mix cheese with chives. Shape into a ball with butter paddles. Arrange ball in centre of pineapple, garnish with pimiento cut in very thin strips. Serve with mayonnaise to which an equal quantity of whipped cream has been added. Servings, 1."
