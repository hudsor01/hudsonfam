prepTime: ""
cookTime: "2 1/2 hours"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups bread sponge"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "2 cups sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon soda, dissolved in 1/2 cup water"
  - "1 cup lard and butter mixed"
  - "1 teaspoon cinnamon"
  - "1 teaspoon cloves"
  - "1 teaspoon nutmeg"
  - "2 cups raisins"
  - "1 cup mixed fruit (figs, orange peel, cherries, citron)"
  - "3 1/2 cups flour"
instructions:
  - "Mix all ingredients together well and let rise two and one-half hours. Bake in two loaves in a moderate oven for 2 1/2 hours at 350 degrees. This cake is best made two or more months before wanted for use. Wrap in waxed paper, put in a tin box and seal."
