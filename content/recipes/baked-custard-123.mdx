servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "3 egg yolks"
  - "2 egg whites"
  - "1 pint milk"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 teaspoon flavoring"
instructions:
  - "Beat eggs slightly and add to scalded milk with sugar, salt, and flavoring. Pour into custard cups, set in a pan of hot water and bake in a moderate oven. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 325 degrees. Servings, 6."
