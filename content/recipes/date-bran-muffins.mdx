title: "Date Bran Muffins"
category: "Bread"
status: "published"
order: 5401
servings: "12"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "25 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 tablespoon sugar"
  - "2 cups bran"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 1/2 cups milk"
  - "1/2 cup dates"
instructions:
  - "Sift flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Mix with bran into which the beaten egg has been stirred. Add the milk and beat thoroughly, then add dates cut into small pieces. Bake in moderate oven for 25 minutes at 350 degrees."
