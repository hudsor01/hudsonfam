servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "one-half hour"
ingredients:
  - "2 cans string beans"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "Speck cayenne"
  - "1/2 cup grated cheese"
  - "3 tablespoons heavy cream"
  - "Seasoning"
instructions:
  - "Drain beans. Place them on a clean cloth to absorb all moisture. When well dried, place one half in a greased baking dish, season with salt and cayenne, cover with cheese, pour over cream, repeat, having the top layer of cheese. Place in a moderate oven to heat until cheese melts and browns slightly in the oven for one-half hour at 350 degrees. Servings, 8."
