cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 quarts bread-crumbs"
  - "1 cup molasses"
  - "4 teaspoons salt"
  - "4 tablespoons shortening"
  - "2 quarts hot water"
  - "1 yeast cake, dissolved in 2 tablespoons lukewarm water"
  - "1 quart graham flour"
  - "White flour to knead"
instructions:
  - "Dry and grind bread in the meat-chopper before measuring. Add molasses, salt, and shortening to the water and pour over the crumbs. When lukewarm stir in the yeast. Add graham flour and white flour to knead."
  - "Brush with melted fat and let rise overnight. In the morning shape into loaves. Brush with fat and let rise until double in bulk. Bake in a moderately hot oven for 1 hour at 380 degrees."
