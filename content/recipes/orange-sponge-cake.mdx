title: "Orange Sponge Cake"
category: "Cake"

servings: "10"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "40 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "3 eggs"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 cup orange juice"
  - "Grated rind of one orange"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "1 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
instructions:
  - "Beat whites of eggs until stiff, then add the yolks one at a time, beating after each addition. Add the sugar, grated orange rind and orange juice. Fold in the flour mixed and sifted with baking powder and salt and bake in a shallow pan in a moderate oven for 40 minutes at 325 degrees. Cut in fancy shapes."
