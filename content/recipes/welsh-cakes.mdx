cookTime: "12 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "4 cups flour"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup shortening"
  - "1 cup currants"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup evaporated milk"
instructions:
  - "Sift dry ingredients, work in shortening, add currants, beaten eggs, and milk. Mix like biscuits, pat to one-half-inch thickness and cut in squares. Bake in hot oven for 12 minutes at 425 degrees."
