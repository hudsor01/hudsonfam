title: "Chocolate Sponge Cake"
category: "Cake"
status: "published"
order: 7501
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "40 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups brown sugar"
  - "1/2 cup butter"
  - "1 square chocolate"
  - "1/2 cup sour milk"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 cup boiling water"
  - "2 egg whites"
instructions:
  - "Cream sugar with melted butter and chocolate, add milk and beat thoroughly. Add egg yolks, vanilla, flour mixed with soda and boiling water. Reserve a small portion of flour to add after the boiling water, then beat until very smooth and light. Lastly, add beaten whites. This makes a large loaf cake. Bake in moderate oven for 40 minutes at 300 degrees."
