title: "Banana Fritters"
category: "Bread"
status: "published"
order: 4903
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "3 minutes each"
ingredients:
  - "3 bananas"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1/2 cup flour"
  - "1 teaspoon brown sugar"
  - "1/4 teaspoon cinnamon"
instructions:
  - "Peel bananas, which should be very ripe, and press through a sieve. Make a batter of egg, flour, sugar and cinnamon, add banana, beat well. Drop by spoonfuls into hot fat. Fry on both sides until brown. Eat hot with fine sugar. Time in cooking, 3 minutes each at a fat temperature of 380 degrees."
