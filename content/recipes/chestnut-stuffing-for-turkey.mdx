ingredients:
  - "1 quart chestnuts"
  - "2 cups bread or cracker crumbs"
  - "2 teaspoons salt"
  - "1/4 teaspoon pepper"
  - "2 teaspoons chopped parsley"
  - "1/3 cup melted butter"
instructions:
  - "Shell chestnuts, pour boiling water over them and remove inner brown skin."
  - "Boil in salted water or in stock till soft."
  - "Mash fine and add the other ingredients."
