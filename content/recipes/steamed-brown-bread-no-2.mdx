ingredients:
  - "1 cup corn meal"
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup rolled oats"
  - "1 1/2 cups hot water"
  - "3/4 cup molasses"
  - "1 cup white or rye flour"
instructions:
  - "Mix dry ingredients all together except the rolled oats, and sift."
  - "Add the boiling water to oats, also the molasses and shortening."
  - "Combine mixtures and beat thoroughly."
  - "Pour into a well-greased mold, having it only one-half full."
  - "Cook in steamer 3 hours."
