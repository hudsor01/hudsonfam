servings: "4"

cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "1 tablespoon mustard (dry)"
  - "1 tablespoon brown sugar"
  - "1 slice ham"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Mix mustard and sugar with enough water to make a paste. Put ham in baking dish and rub over with paste. Add milk. Bake in a moderate oven until ham is thoroughly done. Bake in the oven for 1 hour at 350 degrees."
