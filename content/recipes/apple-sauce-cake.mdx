title: "Apple Sauce Cake"

order: 6402
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "45 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 cup shortening"
  - "1 3/4 cups flour"
  - "1 cup warm apple sauce"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "1/2 teaspoon orange extract"
  - "1/2 teaspoon cinnamon extract"
  - "1/2 teaspoon nutmeg extract"
  - "1 cup raisins"
instructions:
  - "Cream the sugar and shortening together, add the flour and the warm apple sauce in which the soda has been dissolved; add the extracts, and the raisins which have been dredged in flour. Bake in a loaf in a moderate oven for 45 minutes at 350 degrees."
