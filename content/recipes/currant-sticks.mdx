ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "3/4 cup butter or margarine"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup currants"
  - "1 teaspoon cinnamon"
  - "1/2 teaspoon lemon extract"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Mix together flour, sugar, and salt, and work in shortening. Add eggs well beaten, currants and flavorings. Roll out a third of an inch thick and cut in pieces four inches long and half an inch wide. Bake in a moderate oven for 45 minutes at 350 degrees."
