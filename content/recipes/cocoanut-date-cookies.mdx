ingredients:
  - "1 egg"
  - "1/3 cup cooking oil"
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/3 cup dates, cut in pieces"
  - "1/2 cup cocoanut"
  - "2 teaspoons lemon extract"
  - "3/4 cup milk"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 cup nuts, if desired"
instructions:
  - "Beat egg very light and add shortening, sugar, dates, cocoanut, and flavoring. Mix well."
  - "Add milk and flour which has been sifted with baking-powder and salt."
  - "Drop from teaspoon on greased tin. Bake in a moderately hot oven, in the oven for 12 minutes at 375 degrees."
