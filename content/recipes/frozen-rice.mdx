order: 13804
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "1 tablespoon gelatine"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "2 cups boiled rice"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 cup cream"
  - "1 cup finely chopped walnut meats"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Soften gelatine in milk, and place over hot water until dissolved; then add rice, salt, and sugar. When cool, fold in whipped cream, nut meats, and flavoring. Pack in ice and salt, and let stand for two or three hours."
