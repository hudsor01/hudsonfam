title: "Cracked Wheat Muffins"
category: "Bread"
status: "published"
order: 5603
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup fine cracked wheat"
  - "1/2 cup whole wheat meal"
  - "1/2 cup white flour"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 tablespoon sugar"
  - "1 cup water"
  - "1 egg (beaten)"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "3/4 cup molasses"
instructions:
  - "Mix together all dry ingredients but soda. Add water and egg and stir well. Add soda to molasses and add to first mixture. Beat well before pouring batter in hot muffin pans."
  - "Bake in a moderately hot oven for 35 minutes at 375 degrees."
