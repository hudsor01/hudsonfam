title: "Hamburg Steak Savory"
category: "Meat"
status: "published"
order: 20902
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 pounds round steak"
  - "1 slice salt pork"
  - "Salt and pepper"
  - "6 slices bacon"
  - "1 cup tomato"
  - "Parsley"
instructions:
  - "Put steak and salt pork through grinder and season. Form into flat cakes and dip in flour. Fry very thin slices of bacon until curled and crisp; remove to a platter and keep hot. In the fat brown the steak balls and remove to platter. Pour tomato into frying-pan, stir to blend, and when hot pour over steak balls. Garnish with bacon curls and parsley."
