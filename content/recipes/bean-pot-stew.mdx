---
title: "Bean Pot Stew"
category: "Meat"
status: "published"
order: 21202
servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "4 hours"
ingredients:
  - "1 pound beef (bottom of round)"
  - "1 onion"
  - "2 small carrots (sliced)"
  - "1 small white turnip (sliced)"
  - "1/2 can peas"
  - "4 tablespoons vinegar"
  - "4 tablespoons rolled oats or tapioca"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "3 cloves"
  - "Water"
  - "3 potatoes"
instructions:
  - "Cut meat in cubes, brown with onion in a small amount of fat, and add with carrots, turnips, and peas to greased bean pot."
  - "Add remaining ingredients except potatoes, cover with water, and bake in a moderate oven. Add potatoes the last hour of cooking. Bake in the oven for 4 hours at 350 degrees."
