servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup macaroni"
  - "1 1/2 cups thin white sauce"
  - "1/2 cup grated American cheese"
  - "1/2 cup buttered bread crumbs"
instructions:
  - "Cook macaroni in salted water until soft, and mix with white sauce, and cheese. Pour into greased baking dish, sprinkle buttered crumbs over top of mixture, and bake in moderate oven until crumbs are golden brown, and the sauce cooks up through the mixture, in the oven for 20 minutes at 350 degrees."
