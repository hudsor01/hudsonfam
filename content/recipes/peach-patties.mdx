---
title: "Peach Patties"
category: "Pie"
status: "published"
order: 26202
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 cup peach syrup"
  - "1 slice lemon"
  - "4 cloves"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "1/2 cup shredded cocoanut"
  - "6 canned peach halves"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Dissolve sugar in syrup from peaches. Add lemon, cloves, and butter. Boil five minutes. Moisten cocoanut with a little of this syrup and fill cavities of peaches. Line muffin tins with pastry and place a peach half in each, cut side down. Pour a tablespoon of the syrup over each, and bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 425 degrees, sprinkling with dry cocoanut a few minutes before taking from oven. Garnish with maraschino cherries."
