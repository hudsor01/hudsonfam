title: "Baking Powder Biscuits"
category: "Breads"

  - "/images/recipes/_pages/p42-biscuits-and-rolls.jpeg"
contributor: "Grandma Hudson"
sourceNote: "From Grandma Hudson's copy of the Modern Priscilla Cook Book (Biscuits and Rolls)"
cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups bread flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "About 3/4 cup of milk, water, or half-and-half"
instructions:
  - "Mix and sift the dry ingredients, then work in the shortening."
  - "Add the liquid gradually, making a dough as soft as can be handled."
  - "Turn onto a floured board, pat and roll to about one-half inch thickness, and cut."
  - "Place in a pan and bake in a hot oven."
tags:
  - "biscuits"
dateAdded: "2026-06-01"
---

Bake in a hot oven (about 450°F) for 15 minutes.
