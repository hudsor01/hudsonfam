servings: ""
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour (bread)"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "About 3/4 cup of milk and water, half and half"
instructions:
  - "Mix and sift dry ingredients, work in shortening, add liquid gradually, making a dough as soft as can be handled."
  - "Turn on to a floured board, pat and roll to about one-half inch in thickness, cut, place in a pan, and bake in a hot oven for 15 minutes at 450 degrees."
