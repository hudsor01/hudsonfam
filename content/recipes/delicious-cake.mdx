servings: "12"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup butter"
  - "2 cups sugar"
  - "4 eggs"
  - "3 1/2 cups sifted flour"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 cup water"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1 cup raisins, chopped fine"
  - "1 cup walnut meats"
  - "1 cup citron, sliced thin"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter and sugar. Add egg yolks and beat until creamy."
  - "Sift flour and baking-powder together and add alternately with water to first mixture."
  - "Add vanilla, raisins, nut meats, and citron."
  - "Fold in egg whites beaten stiff."
  - "Bake in two layers in a moderate oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees."
