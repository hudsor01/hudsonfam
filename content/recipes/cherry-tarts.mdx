title: "Cherry Tarts"
category: "Pie"
status: "published"
order: 26002
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup chopped cherries"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "2 egg whites"
  - "4 tablespoons sugar (for meringue)"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Line tart pans with a rich pastry. Fill with a mixture of cherries, sugar, and beaten egg yolks. Bake in a moderately hot oven. Cover with meringue made from egg whites and sugar, and brown delicately in a moderate oven. If white cherries are used, a dash of nutmeg improves the flavor. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 400 degrees 5 minutes, 350 degrees 15 minutes. Time for meringue, 15 minutes at 325 degrees."
