ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup strong coffee"
  - "1 pint bread sponge"
  - "3 tablespoons light brown sugar"
  - "1 cup seedless raisins"
  - "1 egg (beaten)"
  - "1 tablespoon melted butter"
  - "1/2 cup chopped nuts"
instructions:
  - "Stir the lukewarm coffee into the sponge, add the sugar, raisins, beaten egg, melted shortening, and nut meats. Mix thoroughly. Add enough flour to make a stiff cake batter."
  - "Pour into a cake pan or bread tin. Let rise until very light and bake in a moderately hot oven for 1 hour at 380 degrees."
