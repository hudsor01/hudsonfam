ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "3/4 cup peach syrup"
  - "Sliced peaches (canned)"
instructions:
  - "Combine first five ingredients as for baking powder biscuit. Roll out three-eighths inch thick and spread with peaches. Roll and bake in a hot oven. Serve garnished with sliced peaches. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 400 degrees."
