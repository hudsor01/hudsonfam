category: "Pie"
status: "published"

servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 slice pineapple"
  - "1 cup raisins"
  - "1/2 cup water"
  - "3/8 cup sugar"
  - "2 tablespoons cornstarch"
  - "1/2 lemon, juice"
  - "1/2 cup pineapple juice"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Chop pineapple and add to raisins with water. Mix sugar and cornstarch thoroughly and stir into raisin mixture. Cook ten minutes, add lemon and pineapple juice, and a speck of salt."
  - "Cool and then bake between two crusts in a moderately hot oven. The filling may be baked in a deep dish with a baking powder crust and served as a pudding with marshmallow sauce. Bake in the oven for 35 minutes at 400 degrees 5 minutes, 380 degrees 30 minutes."
