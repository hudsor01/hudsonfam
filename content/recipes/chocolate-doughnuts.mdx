title: "Chocolate Doughnuts"
category: "Doughnuts"
status: "published"
order: 4803
servings: "36"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "3 minutes each"
ingredients:
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1/8 cup cocoa or 2 squares grated chocolate melted with 1 teaspoon butter"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "5 1/4 cups flour"
  - "Pinch salt"
  - "1 cup milk"
instructions:
  - "Beat egg. Add sugar and stir thoroughly."
  - "Add cocoa or chocolate."
  - "Sift baking powder and salt into a little flour and add alternately with milk."
  - "Stir in enough flour to make a stiff dough, stiff enough to roll out, using about five cups."
  - "Cut, and fry in deep fat for 3 minutes each at a fat temperature of 380 degrees. A delightful change from the ordinary plain doughnuts."
