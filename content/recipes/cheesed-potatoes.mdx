servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 quart sliced potatoes"
  - "1/4 cup cheese"
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "Salt and pepper"
instructions:
  - "Parboil the potatoes two minutes. Arrange potato and cheese in layers with a dusting of flour on each layer. When dish is full cover with milk to which seasonings have been added. Bake in a moderate oven. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 325 degrees."
