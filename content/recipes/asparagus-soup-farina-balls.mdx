ingredients:
  - "1 can asparagus (small)"
  - "4 cups water"
  - "2 cups stock or consomme"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "Scraping of onion"
instructions:
  - "Cut off the tips from the canned asparagus and save. Boil the stalks in water until quite mushy, about ten minutes, press through a sieve and add more water to make three cups liquid. Add consomme or rich white stock. Thicken with butter and flour and add onion and asparagus tips. Servings, 6."
