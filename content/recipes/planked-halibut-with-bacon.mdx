servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 pound slice of halibut"
  - "4 slices bacon"
  - "Mashed potato"
instructions:
  - "Lay the slice of halibut in a baking pan and cover with the slices of bacon."
  - "Bake until browned on one side and the fish is partly done, then remove the fish to a heated plank, turn the pieces of bacon, and garnish with a border of mashed potato."
  - "Return to the oven or brown under the broiler, then garnish with slices of lemon, parsley, tomato, and cucumber and serve at once. Total time in the oven, 30 minutes at 350 degrees."
