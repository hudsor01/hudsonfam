---
title: "Scalloped Cucumbers"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 33503
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "10 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "3 cucumbers"
  - "1 1/2 cups milk"
  - "3 tablespoons flour"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "Salt, pepper"
  - "3 tablespoons crumbs, buttered"
instructions:
  - "Pare the cucumbers. Cut in quarters lengthwise, then in halves. Cook in a small amount of water. Put in a baking dish, cover with the cooked sauce made with the milk, flour, butter, and seasonings to taste. Cover with crumbs and bake in a moderately hot oven until crumbs are brown in the oven for 10 minutes at 375 degrees. Servings, 6."
