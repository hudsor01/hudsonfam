servings: "10"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup butter or cooking oil"
  - "1 1/2 cups brown sugar"
  - "1/2 cup cocoa"
  - "1 cup hot water"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup buttermilk or sour milk"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter and sugar, add cocoa mixed with one-half of water, and soda in other half."
  - "Add flour with baking powder and salt."
  - "Lastly, beat in sour milk and vanilla."
  - "Bake in layers in a moderate oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. Use Chocolate Filling (see page 197.)"
