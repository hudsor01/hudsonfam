title: "Apple and Cocoanut Tarts"
category: "Pie"
status: "published"
order: 25902
servings: "16"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "25 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup grated apple"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/8 teaspoon cinnamon"
  - "1/4 teaspoon lemon rind"
  - "2 egg whites"
  - "1 cup cocoanut (freshly grated)"
  - "Tartlet shells"
instructions:
  - "To grated apple, add sugar, cinnamon, and grated lemon rind. Add stiffly beaten egg whites. Sprinkle tartlet shells with cocoanut, fill and sprinkle the tops with cocoanut. Put in a hot oven to set the crust; then reduce heat to moderate. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes at 400 degrees 5 minutes, 325 degrees 20 minutes."
