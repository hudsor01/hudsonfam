servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "45 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 cup shortening"
  - "1/2 cup boiling water"
  - "1 cup molasses"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 1/2 cups flour"
  - "1/2 teaspoon ginger"
  - "1/2 teaspoon cinnamon"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 teaspoon soda"
instructions:
  - "Cream sugar and shortening. Add boiling water, molasses, egg, and sifted dry ingredients. Mixture is very thin. Put into a greased pan and bake in a moderate oven in the oven for 45 minutes at 350 degrees."
