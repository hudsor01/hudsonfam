ingredients:
  - "1 cup sour milk"
  - "1/4 cup molasses"
  - "1 teaspoon grated chocolate"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "3 cups graham flour"
  - "3/4 teaspoon soda"
instructions:
  - "Add sour milk to molasses."
  - "Add chocolate, salt, and enough graham flour mixed with soda, to make a soft dough."
  - "Put into a small greased loaf tin and bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour at 325 degrees."
