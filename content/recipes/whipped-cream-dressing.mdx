ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon flour"
  - "2 tablespoons vinegar"
  - "2 lemons (juice)"
  - "2 egg whites"
  - "2 cups whipped cream"
instructions:
  - "Mix sugar, flour, vinegar, and lemon juice. Cook in double boiler until thick. Fold in egg whites, which have been beaten stiff. When cool, add the whipped cream."
