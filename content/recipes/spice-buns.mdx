cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 quart bread dough"
  - "1/2 cup brown sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon allspice"
  - "3 tablespoons butter or lard"
  - "1/2 teaspoon grated nutmeg"
  - "2 tablespoons, each, grated orange and lemon rind"
  - "Flour to knead"
  - "Egg"
  - "Cinnamon"
instructions:
  - "Mix into dough all the ingredients except egg and cinnamon and mold into buns. Let rise until light, brush with egg and sprinkle with cinnamon. Bake a rich brown in a moderately hot oven for 35 minutes at 375 degrees."
