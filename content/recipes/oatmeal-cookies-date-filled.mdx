cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup shortening"
  - "1 cup brown sugar"
  - "1/2 cup sour milk"
  - "4 cups oatmeal"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "1/2 teaspoon nutmeg"
instructions:
  - "Cream shortening and sugar, add sour milk and oatmeal. Sift salt, soda, flour, and nutmeg. Add and mix well. Roll out on a floured board. Cut in rounds, put a teaspoon of filling on top, place another round over filling and press edges together. Bake in a moderate oven for 15 minutes at 350 degrees. Filling is on page 199."
