ingredients:
  - "1 medium sized cabbage"
  - "1 onion"
  - "2 tablespoons melted butter"
  - "Boiling water"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "1 tablespoon flour"
  - "1/2 cup vinegar"
  - "Salt, pepper, paprika to taste"
instructions:
  - "Shred cabbage and onion. Add the butter and enough boiling water to cover. Cook one-half hour, add the remaining ingredients, and bring to the boiling point. Serve hot. Servings, 6."
