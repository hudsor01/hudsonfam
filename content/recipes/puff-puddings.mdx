servings: "8"

cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup water"
  - "1/4 cup shortening"
  - "Pinch salt"
  - "Grated lemon peel"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "2 eggs"
instructions:
  - "Boil water and add shortening, salt, and lemon peel. When boiling add flour all at once and stir over fire until mixture will leave sides of pan. Cool a few moments, then beat in eggs, one at a time, with no previous whipping. Drop mixture by spoonfuls in greased muffin-pans or on oiled paper and bake in a moderate oven for 30 minutes at 325 degrees. Cut slit in side of each and fill with Strawberry Filling (see page 201)."
