servings: "6"

cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "4 tablespoons butter (melted)"
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "1 1/2 cups milk"
  - "1 cup mushrooms"
  - "1/4 cup green peppers"
  - "1 1/2 cups diced breast of cooked chicken"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "2 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "1/4 teaspoon mustard"
  - "1 1/2 teaspoons salt"
  - "1 teaspoon paprika"
instructions:
  - "Make white sauce by blending butter and flour, and adding the hot milk. Cook in double boiler, stirring until thickened."
  - "Sauté mushrooms and add to sauce with minced green peppers and chicken."
  - "Beat egg yolks, add seasonings, and add to rest of mixture. Heat until the boiling point is reached and simmer slowly ten minutes. Serve on toast."
