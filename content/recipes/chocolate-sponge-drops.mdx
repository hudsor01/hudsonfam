title: "Chocolate Sponge Drops"
category: "Cookies"
status: "published"
order: 8201
servings: "24"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "3 eggs"
  - "1/2 cup confectioner's sugar"
  - "1/2 cup flour"
  - "2 tablespoons cocoa"
  - "1/8 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Beat whites of eggs until stiff, then beat in sugar gradually."
  - "Beat egg yolks until thick and add to first mixture."
  - "Sift together flour, cocoa, and salt, and fold into first mixture. Add vanilla."
  - "Drop by teaspoonfuls on a greased baking sheet. Decorate with nut meats or a bit of candied fruit. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 325 degrees."
