servings: "10"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup juice from canned pineapple"
  - "3 tablespoons lime juice"
  - "3 tablespoons lemon juice"
  - "1/4 cup orange juice"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 cup boiling water"
  - "3 cups cold water"
instructions:
  - "Mix the fruit juices."
  - "Dissolve the sugar in the boiling water and boil two minutes; cool and add to the fruit juices."
  - "Add the cold water and pour over cracked ice."
  - "Serve with a maraschino cherry or sprig of mint in each glass. Servings, 10."
