ingredients:
  - "2 cups sugar"
  - "2 squares chocolate"
  - "1/2 cup rich milk"
  - "Speck salt"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1/2 cup almond paste"
  - "1 cup marshmallow"
  - "Powdered sugar"
instructions:
  - "Cook sugar, chocolate, and milk until soft ball is formed in cold water. Add salt and flavoring and cool before beating. Pour half of fudge into a deep buttered tin, spread with paste made from almond paste, marshmallow cream, and powdered sugar. Melt the remaining fudge a little to soften, and pour over the marshmallow paste. Cool on ice before cutting."
