ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup shortening"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "1 cup molasses"
  - "1/2 cup currants or seedless raisins"
  - "1/2 cup seeded raisins"
  - "2 1/2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 teaspoon nutmeg"
  - "1/2 teaspoon cloves"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
instructions:
  - "Melt shortening, add milk, molasses, and fruit. Beat in dry ingredients mixed and sifted. Pour into a well-greased mold and cook in steamer three hours."
