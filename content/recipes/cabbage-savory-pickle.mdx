ingredients:
  - "2 heads white cabbage"
  - "2 green sweet peppers"
  - "2 red sweet peppers"
  - "12 small onions"
  - "1/2 cup salt"
  - "3 pints vinegar"
  - "2 1/2 cups brown sugar"
  - "1/2 cup black mustard seed"
  - "1/2 cup white mustard seed"
  - "2 teaspoons celery"
instructions:
  - "Chop cabbage, peppers, and onions. Add salt. Let stand four hours. Drain, and cover with a hot syrup made of the remaining ingredients. Pack in hot sterilized jars and seal."
