ingredients:
  - "1 egg white"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "2 tablespoons nut meats"
  - "12 crackers"
instructions:
  - "Beat egg white stiff, fold in sugar and add flavoring and nut meats. Spread on crackers and bake in a slow oven until slightly browned and crisp. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes at 300 degrees."
