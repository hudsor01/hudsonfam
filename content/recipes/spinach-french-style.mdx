title: "Spinach, French Style"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 35003
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups cooked spinach"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "3 tablespoons flour"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/8 teaspoon pepper"
  - "1/2 teaspoon mustard"
  - "1 tablespoon brown sugar"
  - "1 1/2 cups milk"
  - "1 cup chopped boiled ham"
  - "1 hard-cooked egg"
  - "1/2 cup buttered crumbs"
instructions:
  - "Arrange the spinach in the bottom of a greased baking dish. Melt the butter and add flour, seasonings, and the milk. Stir until the boiling point is reached and add the ham and egg finely chopped; pour over the spinach, sprinkle with crumbs, and bake in a moderate oven, in the oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. Servings, 6."
