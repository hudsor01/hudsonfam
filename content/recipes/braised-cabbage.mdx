prepTime: ""
cookTime: "about forty-five minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 small onion"
  - "1 tablespoon bacon fat"
  - "1 tablespoon flour"
  - "1 cup water"
  - "1/2 medium sized cabbage shredded"
  - "Salt and pepper"
instructions:
  - "Slice onion and brown in bacon fat, stir in flour, add water and cabbage. Cover and cook until cabbage is done, about forty-five minutes, adding more water as needed. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Servings, 6."
