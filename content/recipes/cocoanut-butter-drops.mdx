title: "Cocoanut Butter Drops"
category: "Cookies"
status: "published"
order: 8203
servings: "36"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup butter or margarine"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 cup shredded cocoanut"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter, add sugar, and cream well together. Add eggs slightly beaten and milk."
  - "Add flour sifted with salt and baking powder. Mix well, add cocoanut and vanilla."
  - "Drop from a teaspoon on greased tins. Bake in moderately hot oven, in the oven for 15 minutes at 375 degrees."
