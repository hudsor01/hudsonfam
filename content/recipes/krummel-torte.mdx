---
title: "Krummel Torte"
category: "Dessert"
status: "published"
order: 15501
servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "45 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "6 tablespoons bread crumbs"
  - "1 cup chopped nuts"
  - "1 cup chopped dates (or 1 1/2 cups raisins)"
instructions:
  - "Beat the eggs until light, add the sugar, mix the baking-powder with the crumbs and add, stirring in the nuts, dates or raisins last. Mix well together, spread in a shallow, oblong pan and bake in a moderate oven. Cut in squares, and serve either plain or with plain or whipped cream. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes at 300 degrees."
