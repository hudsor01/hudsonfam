prepTime: ""
cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "1 yeast cake"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "1/2 cup warm water"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "Flour"
  - "1 tablespoon shortening"
  - "1 1/2 cups mashed potato (sweet)"
  - "1 cup graham flour"
  - "2 cups scalded milk, cooled"
  - "1 1/2 cups raisins"
instructions:
  - "Dissolve yeast in milk and water. Add salt and flour to make a stiff batter. Let rise overnight. In the morning add shortening, potato, graham flour, milk, and flour to make a stiff dough. Let rise until light, add raisins and knead. Then let rise again."
  - "Make into loaves and let rise until double in bulk before baking. Bake for 1 hour at 380 degrees."
