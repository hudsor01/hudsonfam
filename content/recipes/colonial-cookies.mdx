title: "Colonial Cookies"
category: "Cookies"
status: "published"
order: 8401
servings: "48"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "10-12 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "3/4 cup shortening"
  - "1/2 cup granulated sugar"
  - "1/2 cup brown sugar"
  - "1 egg yolk"
  - "1/2 cup sour cream"
  - "3/4 cup rolled oats"
  - "1/2 cup boiling water"
  - "4 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon baking soda"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 cup each raisins and walnuts"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Cream shortening and sugar, add egg yolk, and cream."
  - "Scald rolled oats with boiling water and add to first mixture with flour sifted and mixed with baking soda, salt, fruit and nuts. Add vanilla."
  - "Chill and roll out. Have the dough as soft as can be handled. Using less flour if necessary."
  - "Bake in a moderate oven, in the oven for 10-12 minutes at 350 degrees. Keep in a crock. These cookies improve with age."
