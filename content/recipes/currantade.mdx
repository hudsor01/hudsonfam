ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cups sugar"
  - "2 cups hot water"
  - "1 pint cooked currant juice"
  - "3/4 cup lemon juice"
  - "3 quarts cold water"

  - "Dissolve sugar in hot water, add currant juice, lemon juice and cold water. Chill. A few pineapple cubes may be added, if liked. Seal in quart bottles and keep on ice."
