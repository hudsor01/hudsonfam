title: "Plain Butter Cake"
category: "Cake"
status: "published"
order: 6602
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/4 cup butter or margarine"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "1 1/2 cups flour"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter, add sugar gradually, and egg well beaten. Add milk alternately with flour and baking powder, which have been mixed and sifted. Add vanilla and beat thoroughly. Bake in a loaf in a moderate oven for 35 minutes at 350 degrees."
