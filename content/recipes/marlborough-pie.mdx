servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup grated apple"
  - "1/2 cup granulated sugar"
  - "3 tablespoons butter"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 lemon"
  - "1/2 cup rich milk"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Grate apple. Cream sugar and butter and add egg well beaten. Add juice and rind of lemon, apple, and milk."
  - "Put in a pastry lined pie plate and arrange pastry strips on top. Bake in a moderately hot oven in the oven for 35 minutes at 400 degrees 5 minutes, 375 degrees 30 minutes."
