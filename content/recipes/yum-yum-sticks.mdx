servings: "18"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "3/4 cup melted shortening"
  - "1 egg"
  - "2 ounces unsweetened chocolate"
  - "1/2 cup flour"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/4 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1/4 cup chopped walnuts"
  - "1/4 cup seeded raisins"
instructions:
  - "Mix sugar and shortening, add egg slightly beaten, then chocolate which has been melted and cooled, flour sifted with salt, vanilla, chopped nuts, and raisins cut in halves. Spread evenly on well-greased baking sheet. Bake in a moderate oven, and cut in strips about three inches long and an inch wide, in the oven for 20 minutes at 325 degrees."
