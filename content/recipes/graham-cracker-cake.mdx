servings: "12"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup butter or other shortening"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 cup cocoanut"
  - "1 cup graham cracker crumbs"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter, add sugar, beaten eggs, milk, sifted flour, and baking powder."
  - "Beat well, add cocoanut, and cracker crumbs last and mix in quickly."
  - "Flavor, and bake in two layers in a moderately hot oven for 20 minutes at 375 degrees."
  - "Put the two layers together with Custard Filling (see page 199)."
