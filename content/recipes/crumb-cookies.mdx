title: "Crumb Cookies"
category: "Cookies"
status: "published"
order: 8403
servings: "60"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup shortening"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1/2 cup cold coffee"
  - "1/2 cup molasses"
  - "2 1/2 cups crumbs"
  - "2 1/2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 teaspoon nutmeg"
  - "1 teaspoon lemon extract"
instructions:
  - "Dry cake, cooky, or doughnut crumbs, and put through the food-chopper before measuring."
  - "Cream shortening and sugar, add well beaten eggs, coffee, molasses and crumbs."
  - "Sift flour, soda, salt, and nutmeg and add to mixture. Add lemon extract. Mix thoroughly."
  - "Drop by teaspoonfuls on a greased baking-sheet. Bake in a moderate oven, in the oven for 20 minutes at 350 degrees."
