servings: ""
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "50 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/4 cups water"
  - "2/3 cup corn meal"
  - "1 1/2 teaspoons salt"
  - "1/4 cake yeast, softened in 1/4 cup lukewarm water"
  - "2 1/2 cups flour"
instructions:
  - "Pour the water over the corn meal and salt, and heat to the boiling-point. Cook twenty minutes in a double boiler or over hot water. Cool, add yeast and flour, then knead and let rise until double in bulk."
  - "Shape into a loaf and let rise in the pan until again double in bulk. Bake in a moderately hot oven for 50 minutes at 380 degrees."
