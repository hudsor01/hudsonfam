title: "Cocoanut Cream Pie"
category: "Pie"
status: "published"
order: 25302
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons cornstarch"
  - "1 1/2 cups water"
  - "1/2 cup condensed milk"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup grated cocoanut"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Mix cornstarch with a little cold water. Add rest of water, milk, and salt. Cook in double boiler until thickened, stirring constantly."
  - "Add cocoanut, eggs slightly beaten, and vanilla. Pour into plate lined with pastry and bake in a moderately hot oven until firm in the oven for 30 minutes at 400 degrees 5 minutes, 350 degrees 25 minutes."
