ingredients:
  - "1 pound pork chops"
  - "6 sweet potatoes"
  - "2 medium onions"
  - "3 tablespoons flour"
  - "Salt and pepper"
instructions:
  - "Cut chops in pieces. Slice raw sweet potatoes and onions. Arrange in layers in casserole, sprinkle each layer with flour, salt and pepper. Cover with water. Cook in moderate oven in the oven for 2 hours at 350 degrees."
