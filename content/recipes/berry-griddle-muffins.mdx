category: "Bread"
status: "published"

servings: "18"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "3 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "3/4 cup berries"
instructions:
  - "Sift dry ingredients, rub in shortening and add milk to make a soft batter. Add the berries and drop in muffin rings and bake on a hot griddle. Without the berries these are like baking powder biscuit. Time in cooking, 20 minutes."
