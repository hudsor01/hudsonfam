ingredients:
  - "1 teaspoon granulated gelatine"
  - "3 tablespoons cold water"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1/4 cup hot water"
  - "2 egg whites"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Soak gelatine in cold water and dissolve over steam. Dissolve sugar in hot water and boil until the syrup will spin a thread. Strain gelatine into syrup. Have egg whites beaten very stiff on a platter, add syrup slowly, beating constantly. When all the syrup has been added, turn the mixture into the top of a double boiler and cook until it is stiff enough to hold its shape, stirring all the time with a wooden spoon."
