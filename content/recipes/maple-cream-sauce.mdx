ingredients:
  - "A few grains salt"
  - "1 egg white"
  - "1/2 cup maple sugar"
  - "1 tablespoon white corn syrup"
  - "2 tablespoons water"
  - "1/8 cup thick cream"
instructions:
  - "Add salt to egg white and beat very stiff. Boil sugar, corn syrup, and water to soft-ball stage. Pour over stiffly beaten white of egg, stirring constantly. Cool and fold in whipped cream."
