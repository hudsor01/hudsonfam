ingredients:
  - "2 egg whites"
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "3/4 cup dates, cut in small pieces"
  - "1/4 cup walnuts, chopped"
  - "1/4 cup cocoanut"
  - "1 teaspoon orange extract"
instructions:
  - "Beat egg whites stiff and beat sugar into them. Fold in other ingredients."
  - "Drop from teaspoon on greased tin. Bake in a moderate oven, in the oven for 30 minutes at 300 degrees."
