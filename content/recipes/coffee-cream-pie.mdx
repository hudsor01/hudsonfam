title: "Coffee Cream Pie"
category: "Pie"
status: "published"
order: 25303
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/3 cup ground coffee"
  - "3 cups milk"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "3 tablespoons sugar"
  - "3 tablespoons cornstarch"
  - "Pastry"
instructions:
  - "Tie coffee in a small muslin bag, place in cold milk and let stand twenty minutes. Then cook for five minutes and remove bag."
  - "Beat eggs slightly; add to sugar and cornstarch. Add hot milk and cook in double boiler until thickened."
  - "Fill crust and bake in a moderately hot oven until firm in the oven for 35 minutes at 400 degrees 5 minutes, 350 degrees 30 minutes."
