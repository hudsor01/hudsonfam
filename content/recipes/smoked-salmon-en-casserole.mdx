servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "3/4 pound smoked salmon"
  - "2 cups diced potatoes"
  - "3/4 cup sliced onion"
  - "2 cups peas"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "1/2 cup hot milk"
instructions:
  - "Cut the salmon in half-inch pieces. Cover the bottom of a casserole with the onions and potatoes, add the fish and peas, pour hot water over this, and bake forty-five minutes in a moderate oven."
  - "Make a white sauce of the butter, flour, and milk and add to the casserole. Cook fifteen minutes longer, stirring if necessary. Total time in the oven, 1 hour at 325 degrees."
