ingredients:
  - "1 No. 1 can salmon"
  - "1 tablespoon gelatine"
  - "2 tablespoons cold water"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 teaspoons sugar"
  - "1 1/2 teaspoons mustard"
  - "Few grains cayenne"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "1 1/2 tablespoons melted butter"
  - "1/4 cup vinegar"
instructions:
  - "Remove the salmon from the can, rinse well with warm water to remove the oil, and separate into flakes. Soak the gelatine in the cold water."
  - "Make a boiled dressing: heat the milk in a double boiler and add the salt, sugar, mustard, and cayenne mixed. Add the egg yolks slightly beaten and the melted butter, pour a little of the hot milk over them, then return to the double boiler and cook until thick, stirring constantly."
  - "When thick, add the vinegar and the soaked gelatine. Strain and add to the salmon. When the mixture begins to set, mold."
  - "Serve with Cucumber Sauce (see page 230)."
