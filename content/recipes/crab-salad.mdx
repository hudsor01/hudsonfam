---
title: "Crab Salad"
category: "Salad"
status: "published"
order: 27602
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "1 pound crab meat"
  - "2 cups diced celery"
  - "2 tablespoons vinegar"
  - "Salt, pepper and paprika"
  - "3 tablespoons mayonnaise"
  - "Chicory"
  - "Sliced pickles"
  - "Olives"
instructions:
  - "Flake crab, and add to diced celery, season with vinegar, salt, pepper, and paprika."
  - "Chill thoroughly, mix with three tablespoons mayonnaise, and heap on lettuce leaves."
  - "Garnish with mayonnaise, chicory, sliced pickles, and olives."
  - "Keep cool until served."
