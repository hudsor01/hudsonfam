title: "Candied Carrots"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 33202
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "25 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "6 medium-sized carrots"
  - "1/4 cup brown sugar"
  - "1/2 cup butter"
  - "1/2 cup marshmallow cream"
instructions:
  - "Boil carrots until tender but not mushy. Melt sugar and butter; add marshmallow. When well mixed, pour over carrots in a baking dish and bake in a moderate oven until a delicate brown in the oven for 25 minutes at 350 degrees. This is a sweet entrée which may also be served as a side dish with meat. Servings, 6."
