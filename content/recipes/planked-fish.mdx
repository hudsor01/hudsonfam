servings: "8"

cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "5 pounds haddock"
  - "6 cups mashed potato"
  - "2 cups minced carrots"
  - "4 pickled beets"
instructions:
  - "Bake the haddock in a moderate oven until thoroughly cooked, but still perfectly firm, and remove to a heated plank. The fish may be stuffed or not as desired. Put a border of potato around the edge, using a pastry bag. Garnish with minced carrots and potato roses. Brown slightly and heat through in the oven for 1 hour at 350 degrees, then arrange the pickled beets which have been cut in fancy shapes."
