ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons pimiento cheese"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1/4 teaspoon paprika"
  - "1/2 cup salad oil"
  - "1 tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice"
instructions:
  - "Cream cheese and butter thoroughly, adding paprika, then add salad oil and vinegar alternately, and very slowly, beating with fork, or egg whip constantly. If sealed in glasses this dressing will keep for some time, and will be just as creamy as when first made."
