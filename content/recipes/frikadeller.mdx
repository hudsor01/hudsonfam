servings: "6"

ingredients:
  - "3/4 pound round steak"
  - "1/8 pound suet"
  - "1 small onion"
  - "Salt and pepper"
  - "1 tablespoon flour"
  - "1 cup rich milk"
instructions:
  - "Put the meat, suet, and onion through food chopper twice. Add salt and pepper to taste, and flour. Mix thoroughly."
  - "Gradually add milk, stirring until light. Dip a spoon into hot grease and shape meat into oblong cakes. Sauté slowly on a hot greased skillet, until browned. Serve with vegetables."
