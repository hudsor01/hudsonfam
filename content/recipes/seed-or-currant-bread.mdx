cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups bread sponge"
  - "1/2 cup butter or margarine"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "1 cup currants, or"
  - "2 tablespoons caraway seeds"
instructions:
  - "Take bread sponge and work in the butter and sugar which have been creamed together, add currants or seeds. Let rise in loaf tin until light, then bake in a moderately hot oven for 1 hour at 380 degrees. This is really like cake and is served as such."
