title: "Butterscotch Squares"
category: "Cookies"
status: "published"
order: 8002
servings: "16"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/4 cup butter"
  - "1 cup brown sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "3/4 cup flour"
  - "1 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "1/4 cup pecan meats"
instructions:
  - "Cook butter and sugar together until smooth and well blended. Cool to lukewarm."
  - "Add egg unbeaten, and beat well. Add flour sifted with baking powder, then vanilla and nuts."
  - "Spread in pan lined with paraffin paper. Bake in a moderate oven, in the oven for 30 minutes at 300 degrees. Turn out of pan and cut in squares."
