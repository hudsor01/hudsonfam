prepTime: ""
cookTime: "1 hour"
ingredients:
  - "4 cups flour"
  - "8 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 3/4 cups milk"
  - "1/2 cup raisins"
  - "1 cup chopped nuts"
instructions:
  - "Sift dry ingredients, add egg well beaten, and milk to make soft dough like biscuit dough."
  - "Add raisins and nuts sprinkled with flour."
  - "Turn into well-greased round tins. Let rise fifteen minutes and bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour at 325 degrees."
