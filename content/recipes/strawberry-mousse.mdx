ingredients:
  - "1 cup heavy cream"
  - "1/2 cup powdered sugar"
  - "Few grains salt"
  - "1 cup strawberry purée (fresh strawberries rubbed through a sieve)"
instructions:
  - "Beat cream until stiff, and fold in sugar, salt, and strawberry purée. One-half cup maraschino cherries cut in pieces may be used instead of strawberries. Pack in ice and salt, and let stand for three hours. Servings, 4."
