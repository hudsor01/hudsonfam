servings: "10"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "10 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 cups white corn meal"
  - "1 quart boiling water"
  - "Salt"
  - "Pepper"
  - "Nutmeg"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "Grated cheese"
  - "Melted butter"
instructions:
  - "Cook first five ingredients together for twenty minutes. Beat in egg yolks. Spread one-half inch thick on a moistened pan. When cold cut in two-inch cubes, and place in a buttered dish. Sprinkle with cheese and melted butter, and bake until brown in a moderately hot oven for 10 minutes at 375 degrees."
