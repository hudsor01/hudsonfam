servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons gelatine"
  - "1 cup hot water"
  - "1 pint ginger ale"
  - "1/4 cup lemon juice"
  - "2 tablespoons sugar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 tablespoons Canton ginger (chopped)"
instructions:
  - "Soften gelatine in a little cold water and dissolve in boiling water. Add ginger ale, lemon juice, sugar, and salt. When partly stiffened stir in the chopped ginger. Turn into a shallow pan. When stiff cut in squares and serve in baskets made by tying together four oblong cheese wafers. Serve with a cream dressing. It takes two pairs of hands to make the cheese baskets, one to hold them while the other ties the ribbons. Servings, 6."
