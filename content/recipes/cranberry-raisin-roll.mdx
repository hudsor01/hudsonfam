ingredients:
  - "2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "2 teaspoons sugar"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "Sweet milk"
  - "1 cup cranberries"
  - "1/2 cup raisins"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
instructions:
  - "Mix a dough of ingredients in Part I and roll one-half inch thick. Chop cranberries and raisins, mix with sugar, and spread over dough. Roll up, and lay in a steamer over quickly boiling water and steam one and one-half hours. Serve hot with Lemon Sauce (see page 163)."
