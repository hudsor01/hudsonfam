---
title: "Strawberry Fritters"
category: "Bread"
status: "published"
order: 5001
servings: "12"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "3 minutes each"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup flour"
  - "1/2 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1/8 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 egg"
  - "2 tablespoons milk"
  - "2 teaspoons butter"
  - "12 large strawberries"
instructions:
  - "Sift dry ingredients, add egg beaten very light, milk, and lastly melted butter. Drop berries in batter, coat well, and using a large spoon drop into hot fat. Serve with sugar. Time in cooking, 3 minutes each at a fat temperature of 380 degrees."
