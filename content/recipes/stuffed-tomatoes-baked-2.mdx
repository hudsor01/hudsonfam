title: "Stuffed Tomatoes, Baked (Pineapple and Cucumber)"
category: "Vegetable"
status: "published"
order: 35402
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "6 tomatoes"
  - "1 cup diced pineapple"
  - "1 cup diced cucumber"
  - "1 cup bread crumbs"
  - "1 teaspoon salt and speck of pepper"
  - "1/4 cup grated cheese"
instructions:
  - "Remove the pulp from ripe tomatoes. Mix the pineapple, cucumber, and part of the crumbs. Season with salt and pepper and stuff the tomatoes."
  - "Add finely cut tomato pulp to fill, sprinkle with the remainder of the crumbs and the cheese."
  - "Grease ramekins or custard bowls, place a tomato in each, and bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees, until the tomatoes are soft."
