---
title: "Fig Cake"
category: "Cake"
status: "published"
order: 7002
servings: "12"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 to 40 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons shortening"
  - "Pinch salt"
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "1 teaspoon nutmeg"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "2 cups bread crumbs (fine)"
  - "1/2 cup milk or water"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1 cup flour"
  - "1/4 pound figs (sliced)"
instructions:
  - "Mix shortening, salt, sugar, and nutmeg; beat and stir in the eggs, then the bread crumbs."
  - "Sift the baking powder and flour together and add the figs."
  - "Combine the mixtures and turn the batter into a loaf-shaped or round pan with cone, well greased."
  - "Bake in a moderate oven for 30 to 40 minutes at 350 degrees."
