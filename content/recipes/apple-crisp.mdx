title: "Apple Crisp"
category: "Dessert"
status: "published"
order: 14701
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "8 apples (sliced) about one quart"
  - "1/2 cup water"
  - "1 teaspoon cinnamon"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "3/4 cup flour"
  - "7 tablespoons shortening"
instructions:
  - "Butter a baking dish and fill with apples, water, and cinnamon mixed. Work together remaining ingredients with finger tips until crumbly, spread over the apple mixture and bake uncovered in a hot oven. Serve with whipped cream, plain top milk, maple syrup or Lemon Sauce (see page 164). Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 400 degrees. Servings, 6."
