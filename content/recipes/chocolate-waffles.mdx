ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup cocoa"
  - "1/2 cup hot water"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/4 cup sugar"
  - "2 tablespoons melted shortening"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Mix cocoa to a paste with the water and bring to boiling-point, stirring constantly. Add milk, eggs well beaten, and dry ingredients mixed and sifted. Add shortening and vanilla and beat well. Serve with chocolate sauce, marshmallow cream, or vanilla ice cream."
