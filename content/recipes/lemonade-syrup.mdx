Variations from the book: add grape juice for grape-juice lemonade; add a dozen
sprigs of fresh spearmint to a quart of lemonade for another flavor; for tea
lemonade, add a pint of tea infusion, a quart of ice water, and (if liked) a
fourth cup of orange or pineapple juice to a cup of the syrup. The syrup keeps,
so you can make it in any quantity you wish.
