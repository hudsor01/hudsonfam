ingredients:
  - "1 cup cracker crumbs (very fine)"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "3 egg whites"
  - "1/2 cup nut meats or cocoanut or fruit"
instructions:
  - "Have crackers rolled very fine. Add sugar and egg whites, beaten stiff. Add nut meats, cocoanut, or fruit."
  - "Bake in a moderate oven, as drop cookies, in the oven for 15 minutes at 350 degrees."
