title: "Petit Beef Cutlets"
category: "Meat"
status: "published"
order: 21203
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "2 1/4 hours"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 pounds beef (cut from neck)"
  - "1/2 cup fine bread crumbs"
  - "1 quart water"
  - "3 whole cloves"
  - "2 teaspoons salt"
  - "1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce"
  - "5 onions"
instructions:
  - "Cut beef in inch cubes, brown quickly in hot skillet. Add crumbs and stir until meat is well covered."
  - "Add water and seasonings and boil five minutes. Place whole onions in casserole or bean pot, pour meat over them and cover closely."
  - "Cook on top of stove, in moderate oven, or fireless cooker until tender. Serve with potato balls and tiny carrots. Bake in the oven for 2 1/4 hours at 325 degrees."
