servings: "20"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: ""
ingredients:
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 pint water"
  - "3 lemons"
  - "1 quart sweet cider"
  - "1 pint grape juice"
instructions:
  - "Place the sugar in the water and boil for about ten minutes, adding a little of the outer lemon rind. When cold add lemon juice, then add the sweet cider and place in a freezer and freeze to a mushy consistency. When frozen mix in the grape juice. Serve in tall glasses. Dilute with a little cold water if too strong."
