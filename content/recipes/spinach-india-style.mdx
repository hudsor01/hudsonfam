servings: "4"

ingredients:
  - "1 tablespoon fat"
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/2 teaspoon curry powder"
  - "1/8 teaspoon pepper"
  - "1 cup milk"
  - "2 cups cooked spinach"
  - "3 hard-cooked eggs"
instructions:
  - "Melt the fat and add flour, seasonings, and milk. Bring to the boiling point, stirring constantly. Arrange the hot spinach in a serving dish and slice the eggs over the top. Pour on the hot sauce and serve at once. Servings, 4."
