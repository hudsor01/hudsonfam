title: "Cape Elizabeth Cookies"
category: "Cookies"

servings: "42"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "8 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup butter or margarine"
  - "1 cup brown sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 1/2 cups flour"
  - "1/4 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "3/4 teaspoon cinnamon"
  - "1/4 teaspoon allspice"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup rolled oats"
  - "5 tablespoons milk"
  - "1/2 cup pineapple, cut in shreds"
  - "1/2 cup seedless raisins"
  - "1/4 cup candied orange peel, chopped"
  - "1/2 cup walnut meats, cut in pieces"
instructions:
  - "Cream shortening, add sugar, and cream together. Add egg well beaten."
  - "Mix and sift flour, soda, baking-powder, spices, and salt, then add rolled oats, and add to first mixture alternately with milk."
  - "Mix fruit and nuts with a little extra flour and add to dough. The pineapple should be well drained."
  - "Drop by spoonfuls on greased baking sheet. Bake in a moderately hot oven, in the oven for 8 minutes at 375 degrees."
