---
title: "Chocolate Cream Pie"
category: "Dessert"
status: "published"
order: 12104
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup butter"
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "2/3 cup sweet milk"
  - "4 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "2 egg whites (stiffly beaten)"
  - "1 pint cream"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter and sugar, add the milk, then the baking powder and flour sifted together, and last fold in the egg whites. Bake in a moderate oven, cool and split. Fill with the cream beaten stiff, sweetened, and flavored with vanilla. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. Servings, 6."
  - "Top for pie: 2 squares chocolate, 1/3 cup hot water, 1/2 cup sugar. Melt chocolate in the water to form a thick paste. When blended add sugar and spread on cake."
