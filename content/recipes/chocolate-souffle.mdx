title: "Chocolate Souffle"
category: "Dessert"
status: "published"
order: 15101
servings: "4"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "35 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "2 tablespoons flour"
  - "3/4 cup milk"
  - "2 tablespoons butter"
  - "1 1/2 squares chocolate"
  - "1/3 cup sugar"
  - "2 tablespoons hot water"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1/2 teaspoon vanilla"
instructions:
  - "Make a white sauce of the flour, milk, and butter and add to it the melted chocolate, sugar, and water. Add the beaten egg yolks and cook a few minutes longer. Remove from fire and fold into the beaten egg whites. Add vanilla. Pour into a buttered baking dish and bake until firm in a moderate oven for 35 minutes at 300 degrees."
