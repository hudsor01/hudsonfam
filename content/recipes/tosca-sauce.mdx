ingredients:
  - "5 tablespoons butter"
  - "3 tablespoons flour"
  - "1 cup chicken stock"
  - "1/2 cup cream"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1/4 teaspoon paprika"
  - "2 egg yolks"
  - "1/2 teaspoon lemon juice"
instructions:
  - "Melt three tablespoons butter, add flour and when well mixed, add slowly chicken stock and cream. Stir until boiling point is reached, add salt, paprika, and just before serving add egg yolks slightly beaten, lemon juice, and the remaining two tablespoons of butter in small pieces."
