ingredients:
  - "A brace or more partridges"
  - "Flour"
  - "Salt and white pepper"
  - "1/2 cup small cubes fat pork"
  - "Butter"
  - "2 cups heavy cream"
  - "Dash paprika"
  - "1 cup mushrooms"
instructions:
  - "Cut up birds as for fried chicken."
  - "Dip in flour seasoned with salt and pepper, and brown delicately in fat tried out of pork, to which an equal quantity of butter has been added."
  - "Remove partridge, add cream and season sauce in pan to taste, then add mushrooms."
  - "Thicken sauce if necessary with blended flour and fat."
  - "Replace partridge and reheat. Serve with buttered toast."
