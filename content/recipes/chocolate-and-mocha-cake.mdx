servings: "12"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "30 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/2 cup butter"
  - "1 1/2 cups sugar"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1/2 cup sour milk"
  - "1/2 teaspoon soda"
  - "2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
  - "2 squares chocolate"
  - "1/2 cup boiling water"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter and sugar. Add beaten eggs, milk, flour mixed and sifted with the soda."
  - "Add vanilla then the chocolate, melted, to which hot water has been added."
  - "Bake in layer tins in a moderate oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. Use Mocha Filling and Icing. (see page 201)."
