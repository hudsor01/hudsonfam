  - "Thinly sliced onions"
  - "Pepper and salt"
  - "Butter"
  - "Cream or evaporated milk"
instructions:
  - "Steam or parboil sweet potatoes, peel, and slice a layer into a baking pan. Sprinkle with salt, cover with a layer of thinly sliced onions, and season with pepper and salt. Continue alternately with potatoes and onions. Then cover the top with crumbs and dot with butter. Pour over all a cup or more of cream or evaporated milk. Cover and bake in a moderate oven until the onions are tender, in the oven for 40 minutes at 325 degrees."
