servings: "18"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "12 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/4 cup butter"
  - "3/4 cup sugar"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1/4 cup sour cream"
  - "1/4 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2 cups flour"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter, add sugar, egg well beaten and the sour cream. Mix and sift dry ingredients and add to first mixture. Roll to a quarter of an inch in thickness and cut in small rounds. On half the rounds put a bit of jelly or raisin filling or half a stewed prune. Cover with remaining rounds and press edges together and bake in a moderately hot oven for 12 minutes at 375 degrees."
