title: "Almond Cakes In Paper Cases"
category: "Cake"
status: "published"
order: 7701
servings: "8"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "20 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1/4 cup butter"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
  - "4 egg yolks"
  - "1/4 cup milk"
  - "7/8 cup flour"
  - "1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder"
  - "Shredded almonds"
  - "Powdered sugar"
instructions:
  - "Cream butter, add sugar gradually, egg yolks well beaten, milk, and flour mixed with baking powder. Put in paper cases, sprinkle with shredded almonds and powdered sugar and bake in a moderately hot oven for 20 minutes at 375 degrees."
