---
title: "Pineapple Meringue"
category: "Dessert"
status: "published"
order: 13103
servings: "6"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "10 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup sugar"
  - "1 cup pineapple juice"
  - "1 tablespoon lemon juice"
  - "4 cloves"
  - "Few shreds lemon peel and crystallized ginger"
  - "6 slices canned pineapple"
  - "2 egg whites"
  - "1/2 cup sugar"
instructions:
  - "Dissolve sugar in pineapple juice, add lemon juice, cloves, lemon peel and ginger. Bring to a boil, add pineapple slices and boil fifteen minutes. Remove pineapple and drain. Beat egg whites until stiff, add sugar and continue beating until thoroughly blended. Arrange meringue on pineapple slices, using a pastry bag if desired. Bake in a moderate oven until meringue is firm in the oven for 10 minutes at 325 degrees. Serve cold with the pineapple syrup. Servings, 6."
