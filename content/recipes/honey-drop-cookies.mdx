title: "Honey Drop Cookies"
category: "Cookies"

servings: "24"
prepTime: ""
cookTime: "15 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "3/4 cup honey"
  - "1/4 cup shortening"
  - "1/2 teaspoon cinnamon"
  - "1/4 teaspoon cloves"
  - "2 tablespoons water"
  - "1 egg"
  - "1 1/2 to 2 cups flour"
  - "1 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1/4 teaspoon soda"
  - "1 cup raisins, cut fine"
instructions:
  - "Warm the honey and shortening until melted. While the mixture is warm add the spices. When cold add the water, the beaten egg, and one-and-one-half cups of flour sifted with the baking-powder and soda. Finally stir in the raisins. Sufficient flour must be added to make a dough stiff enough to drop from a spoon. Bake in a moderately hot oven for 15 minutes at 375 degrees."
