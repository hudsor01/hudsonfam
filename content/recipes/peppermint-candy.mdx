ingredients:
  - "2 cups granulated sugar"
  - "3/4 cup boiling water"
  - "1 tablespoon butter"
  - "1 tablespoon vinegar"
  - "1/4 teaspoon soda"
  - "1/4 teaspoon peppermint extract"
instructions:
  - "Stir together all ingredients except extract, let boil until it cracks when dropped in cold water. Add extract and beat until creamy. Pour into a buttered tin, cut into squares."
